2.1
Distance, Displacement, Speed & Velocity
距离、位移、速率与速度
⌄
🔵 English
Distance = length of path travelled by an object.
Displacement = distance between initial and final position in a specified direction (straight line joining start and end).
Speed = distance travelled ÷ time taken (rate of change of distance).
Velocity = displacement ÷ time taken (rate of change of displacement).
Speed and distance are scalar. Velocity and displacement are vector.
🟢 中文
距离 = 物体行走路径的长度。
位移 = 初位置到末位置的直线距离（有方向）。
速率 = 距离 ÷ 时间（距离的变化率）。
速度 = 位移 ÷ 时间（位移的变化率）。
速率与距离是标量；速度与位移是矢量。
Key Formulas / 关键公式
Speed = d/t
Distance / Time
距离 / 时间
v = s/t
Displacement / Time
位移 / 时间
Example 1 — Ahmad runs P→Q→R, total time = 50 s
P to Q = 60 m, then Q to R = 100 m (back toward P). Find: (a) distance, (b) speed, (c) displacement, (d) velocity.
P到Q = 60 m，Q到R = 100 m（折回P方向）。求：(a)距离 (b)速率 (c)位移 (d)速度
(a) Distance = 60 + 100 = 160 m (b) Speed = 160 ÷ 50 = 3.2 m s⁻¹ (c) Displacement = straight line RP = –40 m (toward P) (d) Velocity = –40 ÷ 50 = –0.8 m s⁻¹
Example 2 — Cycling A→B→C→D, time = 20 s (path: 30+30+40+30 m)
Find: (a) distance, (b) average speed, (c) displacement, (d) average velocity.
求：(a)距离 (b)平均速率 (c)位移 (d)平均速度
(a) 30+30+40+30 = 130 m (b) 130÷20 = 6.5 m s⁻¹ (c) Displacement AD = √(30²+40²) = 50 m (d) 50÷20 = 2.5 m s⁻¹

2.1
Uniform Velocity, Acceleration & Equations of Motion
匀速、加速度与线性运动方程
⌄
🔵 English
Uniform velocity: same velocity in equal time intervals.
Acceleration = rate of change of velocity. If velocity increases → positive acceleration. If decreases → negative acceleration (deceleration).
🟢 中文
匀速：在相同时间间隔内速度相同。
加速度 = 速度的变化率。速度增加→正加速度；速度减小→负加速度（减速）。
5 Equations of Linear Motion / 线性运动方程

s = ½(u+v)t
Displacement

s = ut + ½at²
Displacement

v² = u² + 2as
Without time
不含时间
u = initial velocity 初速度 | v = final velocity 末速度 | a = acceleration 加速度 | s = displacement 位移 | t = time 时间
Example 3 — Car from rest, v = 15 m s⁻¹ after 3 s
Find (a) acceleration, (b) displacement.
求：(a)加速度 (b)位移
u=0, v=15, t=3 (a) a = (15−0)/3 = 5 m s⁻² (b) s = ½(0+15)×3 = 22.5 m
Example 4 — Van: u = 8 m s⁻¹, a = 4 m s⁻², t = 5 s
Find (a) final velocity, (b) displacement in 5 s.
求：(a)末速度 (b)5秒内位移
(a) v = 8+(4)(5) = 28 m s⁻¹ (b) s = (8)(5)+½(4)(5²) = 40+50 = 90 m
Example 5 — Ticker Timer (50 dots/s). First gap 0.5 cm, last gap 1.5 cm
Find the acceleration of the trolley (dots 1 to 6).
求小车加速度（第1至第6点）
u = 0.5÷0.02 = 25 cm s⁻¹ v = 1.5÷0.02 = 75 cm s⁻¹ t = (6−1)×0.02 = 0.1 s a = (75−25)÷0.1 = 500 cm s⁻²

2.2
Linear Motion Graphs (s-t and v-t)
线性运动图表（位移-时间图 和 速度-时间图）
⌄
🔵 Displacement-Time Graph (s-t)
• Gradient = velocity.
• Horizontal line (zero gradient) → stationary (v = 0).
• Positive gradient → uniform positive velocity.
• Negative gradient → uniform negative velocity (returning).
🟢 位移-时间图（s-t）
• 斜率 = 速度。
• 水平线（零斜率）→ 静止（v = 0）。
• 正斜率 → 匀速正方向运动。
• 负斜率 → 匀速负方向运动（返回）。
🔵 Velocity-Time Graph (v-t)
• Gradient = acceleration.
• Area under graph = displacement.
• Horizontal line → uniform velocity (a = 0).
• Positive slope → uniform acceleration.
• Negative slope → deceleration.
🟢 速度-时间图（v-t）
• 斜率 = 加速度。
• 图下面积 = 位移。
• 水平线 → 匀速（a = 0）。
• 正斜率 → 匀加速。
• 负斜率 → 减速。
Example 6 — s-t graph: AB (0–20 s), BC (20–50 s), CD (50–100 s)
AB: 0→100 m in 20 s. BC: stationary at 100 m. CD: 100→200 m in 50 s. Find velocities of each segment.
AB：0→100m用20s。BC：静止在100m。CD：100→200m用50s。求各段速度。
AB: v = 100÷20 = 5 m s⁻¹ (uniform velocity) BC: v = 0 m s⁻¹ (at rest) CD: v = 100÷50 = 2 m s⁻¹ (uniform velocity)
Example 7 — s-t graph: OA (0–2 s, +10 m), AB (2–6 s), BC (6–10 s, −10 m)
Find velocities of OA, AB, BC and (b) average speed and velocity for whole journey.
求OA、AB、BC各段速度，以及全程平均速率和速度。
OA: v = 10÷2 = 5 m s⁻¹ AB: v = 0 m s⁻¹ (horizontal) BC: v = −10÷4 = −2.5 m s⁻¹ Avg speed: 20÷10 = 2 m s⁻¹ Avg velocity: 0÷10 = 0 m s⁻¹ (returned to start)

2.3
Free Fall Motion
自由落体运动
⌄
🔵 English
• Gravitational acceleration, g = 9.81 m s⁻².
• Free fall: motion influenced only by gravitational force. No air resistance.
• Falling down: acceleration = 9.81 m s⁻².
• Thrown upward: deceleration = 9.81 m s⁻² (gravitational force slows it down).
🟢 中文
• 重力加速度 g = 9.81 m s⁻²。
• 自由落体：运动只受重力影响，无空气阻力。
• 向下落：加速度 = 9.81 m s⁻²。
• 向上抛：减速度 = 9.81 m s⁻²（重力使其减速）。
Example 8 — Ball released from building, reaches floor in 4 s
Find (a) height of building, (b) velocity at floor. [g = 9.81 m s⁻², u = 0]
求：(a)建筑物高度 (b)落地速度。[g = 9.81 m s⁻², u = 0]
(a) s = ut + ½gt² = 0+(½)(9.81)(4²) = 78.48 m (b) v = u + gt = 0+9.81×4 = 39.24 m s⁻¹

2.4
Inertia (Newton's First Law)
惯性（牛顿第一定律）
⌄
🔵 English
Newton's First Law: An object will remain at rest or move at uniform velocity if no external force acts on it.
Inertia = tendency of an object to remain at rest or continue its uniform motion in a straight line.
Inertia depends on mass. Greater mass → greater inertia.
🟢 中文
牛顿第一定律：如果没有外力作用，物体将保持静止或匀速直线运动。
惯性 = 物体保持静止或匀速直线运动的趋势。
惯性取决于质量。质量越大，惯性越大。
🔵 Negative Effects & Reduction
Front collision: Passengers thrown forward (high speed stop). → Safety belt + Airbag.
Rear collision: Head moves backward. → Headrest at car seats.
🟢 惯性的负面影响与解决方法
前方碰撞：乘客向前冲（急停时）。→ 安全带 + 气囊。
追尾碰撞：头部向后甩。→ 座椅头枕。
📝 Examples of Inertia / 惯性例子
Bus stops → passengers thrown forward 公车急停→乘客向前冲 Card pulled → coin falls into glass 拉纸板→硬币掉入杯中 Lorry harder to stop than motorcycle 卡车比摩托车更难停下

2.5
Momentum & Conservation of Momentum
动量与动量守恒
⌄
🔵 English
Momentum, p = mv. Unit: kg m s⁻¹. Vector quantity.
Principle of Conservation of Momentum: Total momentum before collision = Total momentum after collision (no external force).
m₁u₁ + m₂u₂ = m₁v₁ + m₂v₂
🟢 中文
动量 p = mv。单位：kg m s⁻¹。矢量。
动量守恒定律：无外力作用时，碰撞前总动量 = 碰撞后总动量。
m₁u₁ + m₂u₂ = m₁v₁ + m₂v₂
Example 9 — Bullet (20 g, 100 m s⁻¹) hits block (2.48 kg, at rest)
Bullet embeds in block. Find common velocity.
子弹嵌入木块，求共同速度。
Before: p = 0.02×100 + 0 = 2 kg m s⁻¹ After: p = (0.02+2.48)v = 2.5v 2 = 2.5v → v = 0.8 m s⁻¹
Example 10 — Pistol (2.0 kg) fires bullet (40 g, 200 m s⁻¹)
(a) Why does pistol recoil? (b) Find recoil velocity.
(a) 为什么手枪会后坐？(b) 求后坐速度。
Before: total p = 0 After: 0 = (2.0×v) + (0.04×200) 0 = 2v + 8 v = −4 m s⁻¹ (opposite direction = recoil) Recoil velocity = 4 m s⁻¹
🔵 Application — Rocket Launch
Before launch: total momentum = 0.
Hot gases expelled downward → rocket moves upward (equal but opposite momentum).
0 = momentum of hot gas (down) + momentum of rocket (up)
🟢 应用——火箭发射
发射前：总动量 = 0。
高温气体向下喷出 → 火箭向上运动（大小相同，方向相反的动量）。
0 = 气体动量（向下）+ 火箭动量（向上）

2.6
Force (Newton's Second Law)
力（牛顿第二定律）
⌄
🔵 English
Newton's Second Law: Rate of change of momentum is directly proportional to the force.
F = ma. F in N, m in kg, a in m s⁻².
a ∝ F (directly proportional to force).
a ∝ 1/m (inversely proportional to mass).
🟢 中文
牛顿第二定律：动量变化率与所受的力成正比。
F = ma。F单位N，m单位kg，a单位m s⁻²。
a ∝ F（加速度与力成正比）。
a ∝ 1/m（加速度与质量成反比）。
Formula / 公式
F = ma
Force = mass × acceleration
力 = 质量 × 加速度
a = F/m
Acceleration
加速度
Example 11 — Mass 400 g, Force 5 N → find acceleration
a = F/m = 5÷0.4 = 12.5 m s⁻²
Example 12 — Mass 1.2 kg, a = 25 m s⁻² → find force
F = ma = 1.2×25 = 30 N
Example 13 — Bus 2 400 kg, u = 30 m s⁻¹, stops in 10 s
Find (a) deceleration, (b) braking force.
求：(a)减速度 (b)制动力
(a) a = (0−30)÷10 = −3 m s⁻² (deceleration 3 m s⁻²) (b) F = 2400×(−3) = −7 200 N

2.7
Impulse and Impulsive Force
冲量与冲击力
⌄
🔵 English
Impulse = change of momentum = Ft = mv − mu. Unit: N s or kg m s⁻¹.
Impulsive force, F = (mv−mu)/t. Force acting on an object in a very short time.
Newton's Third Law: For every action there is an equal and opposite reaction.
🟢 中文
冲量 = 动量变化量 = Ft = mv − mu。单位：N·s 或 kg m s⁻¹。
冲击力 F = (mv−mu)/t。在极短时间内作用于物体的力。
牛顿第三定律：每个作用力都有一个大小相等、方向相反的反作用力。
Impulse Formulas / 冲量公式
J = Ft
Impulse = Force × time
冲量 = 力 × 时间
J = mv−mu
Change in momentum
动量变化量
F = (mv−mu)/t
Impulsive force
冲击力
Example 14 — Car 1 200 kg, u = 5 m s⁻¹ hits tree, stops in 0.08 s
Find (a) impulse, (b) impulsive force.
求：(a)冲量 (b)冲击力
(a) J = m(v−u) = 1200×(0−5) = −6 000 kg m s⁻¹ (b) a = (0−5)÷0.08 = −62.5 m s⁻² F = 1200×(−62.5) = −75 000 N
Example 15 — Ball 500 g, u = 0, v = 40 m s⁻¹ in 0.2 s
Find (a) impulsive force, (b) impulse.
求：(a)冲击力 (b)冲量
(a) a = (40−0)÷0.2 = 200 m s⁻² F = 0.5×200 = 100 N (b) J = Ft = 100×0.2 = 20 N s

(a) Stone: 300 g   (b) Car: 1 500 kg
(a) 石头：300 g   (b) 汽车：1 500 kg
