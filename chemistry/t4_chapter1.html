Variable Type / 变量类型 Definition / 定义
Manipulated variable
操纵变量		 The variable that is deliberately changed / controlled by the experimenter. 由实验者刻意改变/控制的变量。
Responding variable
回应变量		 The variable that changes as a result of the manipulated variable. 因操纵变量改变而发生变化的变量。
Fixed variable
固定变量		 The variable that is kept constant throughout the experiment. 在整个实验中保持不变的变量。

1.3
Storage of Chemicals
化学品储存方法
⌄
Chemical / 化学品 Storage Method / 储存方法
Reactive metals
活泼金属		 In paraffin oil. 储存于石蜡油中。
Volatile and inflammable liquids
挥发性和易燃液体		 In a shady area far from sunlight and heat source. 储存于远离阳光和热源的阴凉处。
Substances easily decomposed by light
易感光分解的物质		 In dark bottles. 储存于深色瓶中。
Corrosive chemicals
腐蚀性化学品		 In a cabinet which is kept locked. 储存于上锁的橱柜中。
Heavy metals and toxic substances
重金属和有毒物质		 In a special labelled container in a locked room free from heat. 储存于标有标签的特殊容器中，置于远离热源的上锁房间。
🗑️
1.3
Disposal of Chemicals
化学品处置方法
⌄
Chemical / 化学品 Disposal Method / 处置方法
H₂O₂ low concentration
低浓度双氧水		 Poured directly into the sink. 直接倒入水槽。
H₂O₂ high concentration
高浓度双氧水		 Dilute with water and add sodium sulphite before pouring into the sink. 用水稀释并加入亚硫酸钠后再倒入水槽。
Solid, organic & hydrocarbon waste
固体、有机物及烃类废物		 Kept in special containers. 存放于特殊容器中。
Strong acid & alkali waste
强酸强碱废物		 Kept in closed labelled containers. 存放于有标签的密封容器中。
Solutions with heavy metals & toxic substances
含重金属及有毒物质的溶液		 Kept in plastic bags and evaporated in fume chamber. Heavy metals disposed according to standard procedures. 装入塑料袋并在通风橱中蒸发。重金属按标准程序处置。
Volatile substance waste
挥发性物质废物		 Kept in a closed container away from sunlight and heat. 存放于远离阳光和热源的密封容器中。

1.1 Chemistry = study of structures, properties, compositions and interactions between matter.

1.1 日常生活中的化学品：食品（调味剂、甜味剂、防腐剂）· 医药（镇痛药、抗菌剂）· 农业（肥料、农药）· 工业（油漆、玻璃）
1.1 Careers in Chemistry: Pharmaceutical · Cosmetic · Nanotechnology · Green Technology · Biotechnology
1.1 化学相关职业：制药 · 化妆品 · 纳米技术 · 绿色技术 · 生物技术
1.2 Scientific Method (10 steps): Observe → Infer → Identify Problem → Hypothesis → Identify Variables → Control Variables → Plan Experiment → Collect Data → Interpret Data → Conclude → Report

1.3 储存：活泼金属→石蜡油 · 易燃液体→阴凉远热 · 感光物质→深色瓶 · 腐蚀性→上锁橱柜 · 重金属/有毒→标签容器，上锁房间。
