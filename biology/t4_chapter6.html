Cell Division

Karyokinesis, Cytokinesis, Cell Cycle, Mitosis, Meiosis and Issues of Cell Division on Human Health.

核分裂、胞质分裂、细胞周期、有丝分裂、减数分裂及细胞分裂对人体健康的影响。

🔬
6.1
Cell Division — 2 Stages
细胞分裂——两个阶段
⌄
🔵 Overview
Cell division is divided into two stages: Karyokinesis followed by Cytokinesis.
🟢 概述
细胞分裂分为两个阶段：先是核分裂（Karyokinesis），然后是胞质分裂（Cytokinesis）。
🔴 Karyokinesis
The process of dividing one nucleus of a parent cell into two nuclei.
核分裂——将母细胞的一个细胞核分裂为两个细胞核的过程。
🟢 Cytokinesis
The process of dividing the cytoplasm of a parent cell into two new daughter cells.
胞质分裂——将母细胞的细胞质分裂为两个新子细胞的过程。
Term / 术语 Definition / 定义
Haploid cell
单倍体细胞		 Cell that has one set of chromosomes. 含有一组染色体的细胞。
Diploid cell
二倍体细胞		 Cell that has two sets of complete chromosomes. 含有两组完整染色体的细胞。
Chromatin
染色质		 A mixture of condensed DNA and protein that condenses to chromosomes before cell division begins. 由浓缩DNA和蛋白质组成的混合物，在细胞分裂开始前浓缩成染色体。
Homologous chromosome
同源染色体		 Combination of chromosomes from paternal and maternal chromosomes. 由父方和母方染色体组合而成的染色体。
Paternal chromosome
父方染色体		 A set of chromosomes originated from the male parent. 来自父方（雄性亲本）的一组染色体。
Maternal chromosome
母方染色体		 A set of chromosomes originated from the female parent. 来自母方（雌性亲本）的一组染色体。

6.2
Cell Cycle — Interphase & Phase M
细胞周期——分裂间期与M期
⌄
🔵 Overview
Cell cycle consists of: Interphase (G₁, S and G₂) and Phase M (Mitosis + Cytokinesis) → Two daughter cells.
🟢 概述
细胞周期包括：分裂间期（G₁、S和G₂期）和M期（有丝分裂+胞质分裂）→ 两个子细胞。
G₁ Phase
• Synthesis of protein and cell components (mitochondria, chloroplast).
• Chromosome = chromatin (looks like string).
• 合成蛋白质和细胞组件（线粒体、叶绿体）。
• 染色体=染色质（形似细线）。
S Phase
• DNA synthesis and replication occurs.
• Each chromosome multiplies into two identical sister chromatids joined by centromere.
• DNA合成和复制发生。
• 每条染色体复制为两条相同的姐妹染色单体，由着丝点相连。
G₂ Phase
• Cells grow.
• Cells still have high metabolism and are prepared to replicate.
• 细胞生长。
• 细胞仍具高代谢活性，准备复制。
📝 Cell Cycle Summary
G₁: Protein synthesis 蛋白质合成 S: DNA replication DNA复制 G₂: Cell growth 细胞生长 M: Mitosis + Cytokinesis 有丝分裂+胞质分裂

6.2
Mitosis — 4 Phases (PMAT)
有丝分裂——4个时期（前中后末）
⌄
🔵 Definition
Division process of the cell nucleus into two daughter cells with the same number of chromosomes as its parent — diploid cell (2n).
🟢 定义
细胞核分裂成两个与母细胞染色体数目相同的子细胞的过程——二倍体细胞（2n）。
🔴 Prophase / 前期
• Chromosome is compact, thick, short, twisted — visible under microscope.
• Comprised of sister chromatids joined at the centromere.
• Spindle fibres are formed.
• Nucleolus disappears and nucleus membrane decomposes.
前期：染色体紧密、粗短、扭曲，可在显微镜下观察到。由姐妹染色单体在着丝点处相连组成。纺锤丝形成。核仁消失，核膜分解。
🔵 Metaphase / 中期
• Centromere is arranged in a line at the centre of the cell on the equatorial plane.
• Sister chromatids are tied to each other at the centromere.
• Metaphase ends when the centromere begins to divide.
中期：着丝点排列在细胞赤道板的中央线上。姐妹染色单体在着丝点处相互连接。当着丝点开始分裂时，中期结束。
🟢 Anaphase / 后期
• Centromere divides into two and sister chromatids are separated.
• Spindle fibres shrink and shorten.
• Chromatids are attracted to the opposite pole of the cell.
后期：着丝点分裂为两个，姐妹染色单体分离。纺锤丝收缩和缩短。染色单体被吸引到细胞的相对极。
🟡 Telophase / 末期
• Both sets of chromosomes arrive at opposite poles.
• Nucleolus and nucleus membranes reappear.
• Spindle fibres disappear.
• Chromosomes are shaped again as fine chromatin threads.
末期：两组染色体到达相对极。核仁和核膜重新出现。纺锤丝消失。染色体重新形成细染色质线。
📝 PMAT — Memory Aid / 前中后末
P = Prophase 前期：染色体可见 M = Metaphase 中期：赤道板排列 A = Anaphase 后期：着丝点分裂 T = Telophase 末期：核膜重现

6.2
Cytokinesis — Animal vs Plant Cells
胞质分裂——动物细胞与植物细胞的比较
⌄
🐾 Animal Cells / 动物细胞
Plasma membrane shrinks and forms two daughter cells (cleavage furrow).
细胞质膜收缩，形成两个子细胞（分裂沟方式）。
🌿 Plant Cells / 植物细胞
Vesicle combines in the middle of the cell forming the cell plate. Two daughter cells are formed.
囊泡在细胞中间聚集形成细胞板，形成两个子细胞。
Aspect / 方面 Animal Cell / 动物细胞 Plant Cell / 植物细胞
Similarities / 相似点 Two diploid cells (2n) are formed · Division of cytoplasm occurs · Carries out prophase, metaphase, anaphase and telophase. 两个二倍体细胞（2n）形成 · 发生胞质分裂 · 经历前期、中期、后期和末期。
Centriole / 中心粒 Has a centriole / 有中心粒 Does not have a centriole / 无中心粒
Cell plate / 细胞板 No cell plate during cytokinesis / 胞质分裂无细胞板 Cell plate is formed / 形成细胞板
Location / 发生位置 Mitosis in cell body / 在细胞体发生 Mitosis in meristem tissue / 在分生组织发生
Plasma membrane / 细胞质膜 Constricts during cytokinesis / 胞质分裂时收缩 No constriction / 无收缩

6.3
Meiosis — Definition & Overview
减数分裂——定义与概述
⌄
🔵 Definition
Process of cell division to produce four haploid gametes (n) which contains half the number of chromosomes of the parent cells. Involves two divisions: Meiosis I and Meiosis II.
🟢 定义
产生四个单倍体配子（n）的细胞分裂过程，含有母细胞一半的染色体数目。包含两次分裂：减数第一次分裂（Meiosis I）和减数第二次分裂（Meiosis II）。
Meiosis I Phases / 减数第一次分裂时期
Prophase I — 前期I
同源染色体配对形成二价体（联会），发生交叉互换；纺锤丝形成，核仁和核膜消失。
Homologous chromosomes pair → bivalents (synapsis) → crossing over at chiasmata · Spindle fibres form · Nucleolus & nuclear membrane disappear.
Metaphase I — 中期I
同源染色体排列在赤道板上；着丝点不分离，姐妹染色单体仍相连；每条染色体附着在一极的纺锤丝上。
Homologous chromosomes arranged at equatorial plane · Centromeres do not separate · One chromosome attached to spindle at one pole, its homologue at opposite pole.
Anaphase I — 后期I
纺锤丝收缩拉动同源染色体；同源染色体分离并移向相对极；姐妹染色单体仍在着丝点处相连，作为一个单位移动。
Spindle fibres pull homologous chromosomes apart · Homologous chromosomes separate to opposite poles · Sister chromatids still tied at centromere, move as one unit.
Telophase I & Cytokinesis — 末期I & 胞质分裂
每个极细胞含有一组染色体；纺锤丝消失，核仁和核膜重现；发生胞质分裂。
Each polar cell has a set of chromosomes · Spindle fibres disappear, nucleoli & nuclear membranes reappear · Cytokinesis occurs.
Meiosis II Phases / 减数第二次分裂时期
Prophase II — 前期II
减数第一次分裂产生两个子细胞；每条染色体由着丝点相连的染色单体组成；核仁和核膜消失；每个子细胞形成纺锤丝。
Two daughter cells from meiosis I · Each chromosome = chromatid joined at centromere · Nucleoli & nuclear membranes disappear · Spindle fibres form in each cell.
Metaphase II — 中期II
染色体随机排列在赤道板上；每条姐妹染色单体在着丝点处与纺锤丝相连。
Chromosomes arranged randomly on equatorial plane · Each sister chromatid tied to spindle fibre at centromere.
Anaphase II — 后期II
每条姐妹染色单体分离并移向细胞相对极；姐妹染色单体的着丝点分裂形成子细胞。
Each sister chromatid separates to opposite poles · Centromere of sister chromatid divides to form daughter cells.
Telophase II & Cytokinesis — 末期II & 胞质分裂
纺锤丝消失；核仁和核膜形成；发生胞质分裂，产生四个单倍体配子；这些细胞将成为配子。
Spindle fibres disappear · Nucleoli & nuclear membranes form · Cytokinesis → four haploid gametes produced · These cells will become gametes.
📝 Mitosis vs Meiosis / 有丝分裂 vs 减数分裂
Mitosis: 2 diploid (2n) daughter cells 有丝→2个二倍体子细胞 Meiosis: 4 haploid (n) gametes 减数→4个单倍体配子 Mitosis: 1 division 有丝→1次分裂 Meiosis: 2 divisions 减数→2次分裂 Mitosis: somatic cells 有丝→体细胞 Meiosis: gametes 减数→配子

6.4
Issues of Cell Division on Human Health
细胞分裂对人体健康的影响
⌄
🔵 Causes of Abnormalities
The causes of mitosis and meiosis abnormalities in human health are:
• Physical factors: X-ray, ultraviolet rays and gamma rays.
• Chemical factors: Carcinogenic substances such as formaldehyde, asbestos and tar.
🟢 异常原因
有丝分裂和减数分裂异常对人体健康的影响原因：
• 物理因素：X射线、紫外线和伽马射线。
• 化学因素：致癌物质，如甲醛、石棉和焦油。
🔬 Impact on Mitosis / 对有丝分裂的影响
Uncontrollable mitosis can produce cancer cells. Cancer cells that are not destroyed will divide uncontrollably and form abnormal tumour cells. Tumour cells will attack and destroy adjacent cells as they compete for nutrients and energy for growth.
不受控制的有丝分裂会产生癌细胞。未被摧毁的癌细胞将不受控制地分裂，形成异常肿瘤细胞。肿瘤细胞会攻击并摧毁邻近细胞，以争夺营养和生长所需能量。
🧬 Impact on Meiosis / 对减数分裂的影响
Mutation in the chromosome number occurs when homologous chromosomes or sister chromatids fail to separate during meiosis I or II. There is a loss or addition of chromosomes in the gametes.

Example: Down Syndrome occurs due to the increased number of chromosomes on chromosome 21. The total number of chromosomes becomes 47.
当同源染色体或姐妹染色单体在减数第一次或第二次分裂时未能分离，就会发生染色体数目突变。配子中出现染色体的缺失或增加。

例子：唐氏综合征因21号染色体数目增加而发生，染色体总数变为47条。
📝 Key Points / 关键点
Physical: X-ray, UV, gamma 物理：X射线、紫外线、伽马射线 Chemical: formaldehyde, asbestos, tar 化学：甲醛、石棉、焦油 Mitosis → cancer/tumour 有丝→癌症/肿瘤 Meiosis → Down syndrome (47 chr) 减数→唐氏综合征（47条染色体）

Biology Form 4 Chapter 6 — Cell Division
生物学中四第六章 — 细胞分裂
6.1 Cell division = Karyokinesis (nucleus splits into 2) → Cytokinesis (cytoplasm splits → 2 daughter cells). Haploid (n) = 1 set chromosomes. Diploid (2n) = 2 sets.
6.1 细胞分裂=核分裂（细胞核分裂为2）→胞质分裂（细胞质分裂→2个子细胞）。单倍体（n）=1组染色体。二倍体（2n）=2组。
6.2 Cell Cycle: G₁ (protein synthesis, chromatin) → S (DNA replication, sister chromatids) → G₂ (cell growth) → M phase (Mitosis + Cytokinesis) → 2 diploid daughter cells.
6.2 细胞周期：G₁（蛋白质合成，染色质）→ S（DNA复制，姐妹染色单体）→ G₂（细胞生长）→ M期（有丝分裂+胞质分裂）→ 2个二倍体子细胞。
6.2 Mitosis PMAT: Prophase (chromosomes visible, spindle forms, nuclear membrane breaks) → Metaphase (centromeres line up at equatorial plate) → Anaphase (centromeres split, chromatids to opposite poles) → Telophase (nuclear membrane reforms, spindle disappears).
6.2 有丝分裂前中后末：前期（染色体可见，纺锤丝形成，核膜分解）→中期（着丝点排列在赤道板）→后期（着丝点分裂，染色单体移向相对极）→末期（核膜重建，纺锤丝消失）。
6.2 Cytokinesis: Animal = plasma membrane constricts (cleavage). Plant = vesicles form cell plate. Animal has centriole; plant does not. Animal: mitosis in cell body. Plant: in meristem tissue.
6.2 胞质分裂：动物细胞=细胞质膜收缩（分裂沟）。植物细胞=囊泡形成细胞板。动物有中心粒；植物没有。动物：有丝分裂在细胞体。植物：在分生组织。
6.3 Meiosis = 2 divisions → 4 haploid gametes (n). Meiosis I: homologous chromosomes separate (crossing over in Prophase I). Meiosis II: sister chromatids separate. Key: crossing over → genetic variation.
6.3 减数分裂=2次分裂→4个单倍体配子（n）。减数第一次：同源染色体分离（前期I发生交叉互换）。减数第二次：姐妹染色单体分离。关键：交叉互换→遗传变异。
6.3 Mitosis vs Meiosis: Mitosis → 2 diploid (2n) · 1 division · somatic cells · no crossing over. Meiosis → 4 haploid (n) · 2 divisions · gametes · crossing over occurs.
6.3 有丝分裂vs减数分裂：有丝→2个二倍体(2n)·1次分裂·体细胞·无交叉互换。减数→4个单倍体(n)·2次分裂·配子·发生交叉互换。
6.4 Causes of abnormality: Physical (X-ray, UV, gamma rays) · Chemical (formaldehyde, asbestos, tar). Impact on mitosis → cancer/tumour cells (uncontrolled division). Impact on meiosis → Down syndrome (chromosome 21 non-disjunction, total = 47).
6.4 异常原因：物理（X射线、紫外线、伽马射线）·化学（甲醛、石棉、焦油）。对有丝分裂影响→癌症/肿瘤细胞（不受控分裂）。对减数分裂影响→唐氏综合征（21号染色体不分离，总数=47）。
