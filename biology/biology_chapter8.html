8.1
Adaptations for Gaseous Exchange (4 Key Features)
气体交换的适应特征（4个关键特征）
⌄
🔵 4 Adaptations
1. The thin surface layer of the respiratory structure is just as thick as a single cell.
2. Surrounded by a large network of blood capillaries.
3. The large ratio of total surface area of respiratory structure per volume (TSAV).
4. Moist respiratory structure.
🟢 4种适应特征
1. 呼吸结构的表面层薄如单细胞厚度。
2. 被大量毛细血管网包围。
3. 呼吸结构单位体积的总表面积比率（TSAV）大。
4. 呼吸结构湿润。
📝 Memory — TBMT
Thin surface 薄表面层 Blood capillaries 毛细血管 TSAV large 大表面积比 Moist 湿润

8.2
Mechanisms of Breathing — 4 Organisms
呼吸机制——4种生物
⌄
🦗 Insects / 昆虫
• Tracheal system strengthened with chitin.
• Air flows: spiracle → trachea → tracheole → body cells.
• Some insects have air sacs to increase rate of diffusion of gases.
气管系统由甲壳素加固。气流路径：气门→气管→气管小支→体细胞。部分昆虫有气囊以增加气体扩散速率。
🐟 Fish / 鱼
• Gills consist of smooth filaments with thin projections called lamellae.
• Countercurrent exchange mechanism: water flows opposite to bloodstream → increases diffusion rate.
鳃由平滑鳃丝组成，具有称为鳃瓣的薄突起。对流交换机制：水流方向与血流相反→提高扩散速率。
🐸 Amphibians / 两栖类
• Use lungs and skin (live on land and water).
• Lungs = pair of sacs connected to back of mouth; controlled by the glottis.
• Thin and moist skin surface increases diffusion rate.
使用肺和皮肤（生活于陆地和水中）。肺=一对连接口腔后部的气囊，由声门控制。薄而湿润的皮肤表面提高扩散速率。
🧍 Humans / 人类
• Use lungs. Air enters via trachea (supported by C-shaped cartilage rings).
• Lungs: bronchus → bronchiole → alveolus.
• Millions of alveoli increase surface area for gaseous exchange.
使用肺。空气经由气管（C形软骨环支撑）进入。肺：支气管→细支气管→肺泡。数百万肺泡增大气体交换表面积。
📝 Key Terms / 关键术语
Spiracle → Trachea → Tracheole (insects) 气门→气管→气管小支（昆虫） Countercurrent exchange (fish) 对流交换（鱼） Glottis controls lungs (amphibians) 声门控制（两栖） Bronchus→Bronchiole→Alveolus (humans) 支气管→细支气管→肺泡（人类）

8.3
Gaseous Exchange in Humans — 4-Step Process
人体气体交换——4步过程
⌄
🔵 Overview
Gaseous exchange occurs via diffusion driven by differences in partial pressure of O₂ (PO₂) and CO₂ (PCO₂) between alveolus, blood capillary and body cells.
🟢 概述
气体交换通过扩散进行，由肺泡、毛细血管和体细胞之间O₂分压（PO₂）和CO₂分压（PCO₂）的差异驱动。
4 Steps / 4个步骤
①Alveolus → Blood Capillary (O₂)
In the alveolus, PO₂ is higher → O₂ diffuses into blood capillary.
在肺泡中，PO₂较高→O₂扩散进入毛细血管。
②Blood Capillary → Alveolus (CO₂)
In blood capillary, PCO₂ is higher → CO₂ diffuses into alveolus.
在毛细血管中，PCO₂较高→CO₂扩散进入肺泡。
③O₂ transported in blood
Oxygen joins haemoglobin → forms oxyhaemoglobin → transported to body cells.
氧气与血红蛋白结合→形成氧合血红蛋白→输送到体细胞。在毛细血管中，PO₂较高→O₂分解并扩散进入体细胞。
④Body Cells → Red Blood Cells → Alveolus (CO₂ removal)
PCO₂ is higher in body cells → CO₂ diffuses into red blood cells → then into alveolus to be removed.
体细胞中PCO₂较高→CO₂扩散进入红细胞→再进入肺泡被排出。
🔵 Key Principle
• High PO₂ → O₂ diffuses out (alveolus to blood, blood to body cells).
• High PCO₂ → CO₂ diffuses out (blood to alveolus, body cells to blood).
• O₂ transport: O₂ + haemoglobin → oxyhaemoglobin
🟢 关键原理
• PO₂高→O₂向外扩散（肺泡→血液，血液→体细胞）。
• PCO₂高→CO₂向外扩散（血液→肺泡，体细胞→血液）。
• O₂运输：O₂ + 血红蛋白→氧合血红蛋白
📝 Diffusion Rules / 扩散规则
High PO₂ in alveolus → O₂ in 肺泡PO₂高→O₂进入血液 High PCO₂ in blood → CO₂ out 血液PCO₂高→CO₂进入肺泡 O₂ + Hb → Oxyhaemoglobin 氧合血红蛋白 High PCO₂ in body cells → CO₂ → RBC → alveolus 体细胞→红细胞→肺泡

8.4
Health Issues — COPD & Respiratory Diseases
健康问题——慢性阻塞性肺病与呼吸疾病
⌄
🔵 COPD Definition
Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) is a type of disease that affects the human respiratory system by disturbing the airflow.
🟢 慢性阻塞性肺病定义
慢性阻塞性肺病（COPD）是一种通过干扰气流影响人体呼吸系统的疾病。
🫁 Emphysema / 肺气肿
Alveolus fails to shrink during exhalation. Air is trapped and causes gaseous exchange to become less efficient.
肺泡在呼气时无法收缩。空气被困住，导致气体交换效率降低。
🔬 Chronic Bronchitis / 慢性支气管炎
Inflammation of the bronchial tube results in the destruction of cilia and disturbs the filtering process. A large amount of mucus is produced and bronchial tube space becomes narrow.
支气管发炎导致纤毛被破坏，干扰过滤功能。产生大量黏液，支气管空间变窄。
💨 Asthma / 哮喘
The bronchiole walls become swollen and thick. Air passage becomes narrow, which affects the airflow.
细支气管壁变得肿胀和增厚。气道变窄，影响气流。

8.1 Respiratory organs: Insects=Trachea · Fish=Gills · Amphibians=Skin+Lungs · Humans=Lungs. Gas exchange structures: Insects=Tracheole · Fish=Filament+Lamella · Amphibians=Skin+Alveolus · Humans=Alveolus.
8.1 呼吸器官：昆虫=气管·鱼=鳃·两栖=皮肤+肺·人类=肺。气体交换结构：昆虫=气管小支·鱼=鳃丝+鳃瓣·两栖=皮肤+肺泡·人类=肺泡。
8.1 4 Adaptations for gaseous exchange: (1) Thin surface layer (single cell thick) (2) Surrounded by blood capillaries (3) Large TSAV ratio (4) Moist respiratory surface.
8.1 气体交换的4种适应特征：①薄表面层（单细胞厚）②毛细血管包围③大TSAV比④呼吸表面湿润。
8.2 Insects: Spiracle→Trachea→Tracheole→Body cells. Air sacs increase diffusion. Fish: Countercurrent exchange mechanism (water opposite to blood). Amphibians: Glottis controls lungs; thin moist skin. Humans: C-shaped cartilage rings; Bronchus→Bronchiole→Alveolus.
8.2 昆虫：气门→气管→气管小支→体细胞。气囊增加扩散。鱼：对流交换机制（水流与血流相反）。两栖：声门控制肺；薄湿皮肤。人类：C形软骨环；支气管→细支气管→肺泡。
8.3 Gaseous exchange (4 steps): ① Alveolus: high PO₂ → O₂ diffuses into blood capillary. ② Blood: high PCO₂ → CO₂ diffuses into alveolus. ③ O₂ + Hb → oxyhaemoglobin → transported to body cells. ④ Body cells: high PCO₂ → CO₂ → RBC → alveolus (removed).
8.3 气体交换（4步）：①肺泡：PO₂高→O₂扩散进血液。②血液：PCO₂高→CO₂扩散进肺泡。③O₂+血红蛋白→氧合血红蛋白→输送到体细胞。④体细胞：PCO₂高→CO₂→红细胞→肺泡（排出）。
8.4 COPD = disturbs airflow. 3 diseases: Emphysema (alveolus fails to shrink → air trapped) · Chronic Bronchitis (bronchial inflammation → excess mucus, cilia destroyed) · Asthma (bronchiole walls swollen and thick → narrow air passage).
8.4 COPD=气流受阻。3种疾病：肺气肿（肺泡无法收缩→空气被困）·慢性支气管炎（支气管发炎→过多黏液，纤毛被破坏）·哮喘（细支气管壁肿胀增厚→气道变窄）。
