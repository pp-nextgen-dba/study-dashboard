1. Big Picture 总览

This chapter connects the universe, galaxies, the solar system and stars. 这一章把宇宙、星系、太阳系和恒星联系起来。

Universe 宇宙

What is the universe? 什么是宇宙？

The universe includes everything that exists: galaxies, stars, planets, gas, dust, energy and space.

宇宙包括所有存在的事物：星系、恒星、行星、气体、尘埃、能量和空间。

Galaxy 星系

Main idea 重点

A galaxy is a huge collection of stars, gas and dust held together by gravity.

星系是由引力结合在一起的大量恒星、气体和尘埃所组成的巨大天体系统。

Solar system 太阳系

Location 位置

Our solar system is found in the Milky Way galaxy.

我们的太阳系位于银河系之中。

Hierarchy 层次关系：
Universe → Galaxy → Solar System → Sun → Earth
宇宙 → 星系 → 太阳系 → 太阳 → 地球

2. Galaxies 星系

Definition 定义

Galaxies contain millions or billions of stars together with gas and dust particles.

星系包含数百万甚至数十亿颗恒星，以及气体和尘埃粒子。

Technology such as telescopes helps humans observe distant galaxies. 望远镜等科技帮助人类观察遥远星系。

Types of galaxies 星系的种类

  • Spiral 螺旋星系 – spiral arms; example: Milky Way, Andromeda.
    有旋臂；例子：银河系、仙女座星系。
  • Elliptical 椭圆星系 – oval or round shape; example: Messier 87.
    椭圆或圆形；例子：梅西耶87。
  • Irregular 不规则星系 – no clear regular shape; example: Magellanic Clouds.
    没有固定形状；例子：麦哲伦星云。

3. The Milky Way 银河系

Important

Key facts 重点事实

  • The Milky Way is a medium-sized spiral galaxy. 银河系是一个中型螺旋星系。
  • Our solar system is located at the edge of one spiral arm. 我们的太阳系位于其中一个旋臂的边缘。
  • The Sun is one of billions of stars in the Milky Way. 太阳只是银河系中数十亿颗恒星之一。
Remember 记住

Position concept 位置概念

Earth is inside the solar system, the solar system is inside the Milky Way, and the Milky Way is only one galaxy in the universe.

地球位于太阳系中，太阳系位于银河系中，而银河系只是宇宙中的一个星系。

4. Stars 恒星

Star 恒星

Definition 定义

A star is a hot ball of gases that produces its own light and heat.

恒星是能够自行发光发热的高温气体球。

Sun 太阳

Important fact 重要事实

The Sun is a star, not a planet.

太阳是一颗恒星，不是行星。

Observed properties 观察到的特征

Characteristics of stars 恒星的特征

Stars can be compared by temperature, colour, size, distance from Earth, and brightness.

恒星可以从温度、颜色、大小、与地球的距离、亮度来分类和比较。

5. Star Colour and Temperature 恒星的颜色与温度

Main relationship 主要关系

In general, star colour shows surface temperature. From lower to higher temperature, the sequence is:

一般来说，恒星的颜色反映它的表面温度。从低温到高温，顺序如下：

Red → Orange → Yellow → Yellowish-white → White → Bluish-white → Blue
红 → 橙 → 黄 → 黄白 → 白 → 蓝白 → 蓝

Blue stars are the hottest; red stars are the coolest. 蓝色恒星最热，红色恒星最冷。

Approximate temperature ranges 大约温度范围

  • Red 红色: below 3,500 K
  • Orange 橙色: 3,500 – 5,000 K
  • Yellow 黄色: 5,000 – 6,000 K
  • Yellowish-white 黄白色: 6,000 – 7,500 K
  • White 白色: 7,500 – 11,000 K
  • Bluish-white 蓝白色: 11,000 – 25,000 K
  • Blue 蓝色: more than 25,000 K
Exam tip 考试技巧： Do not confuse brightness with temperature. A star may look dim because it is far away, not because it is cool.
不要把亮度和温度混淆。恒星看起来暗，可能是因为它离地球很远，而不是因为温度低。

6. Life Cycle of a Star 恒星的生命周期

The chapter shows the nebular hypothesis. 本章采用“星云假说”说明恒星的诞生与死亡。

Birth 出生

How stars are formed 恒星如何形成

  • Stars form from nebulae (clouds of gas and dust). 恒星由星云（气体和尘埃云）形成。
  • Strong gravitational force pulls gas and dust together. 强大的引力把气体和尘埃拉向中心。
  • The gas cloud shrinks and becomes denser, forming a core. 气体云收缩并变得更密，形成核心。
  • Hydrogen changes into helium and releases a lot of heat and light. 氢转变成氦，并释放大量热和光。
  • A star is formed. 一颗恒星形成了。
Death 死亡

What happens when a star dies 恒星死亡时会发生什么

  • As hydrogen is used up, changes happen in the star. 当氢逐渐耗尽时，恒星开始发生变化。
  • The star expands and becomes a red giant or supergiant. 恒星膨胀，变成红巨星或超巨星。
  • Small/medium stars end as white dwarfs. 较小或中等恒星最后会成为白矮星。
  • Massive stars may explode as a supernova, then become a neutron star or black hole. 大质量恒星可能爆炸成超新星，最后变成中子星或黑洞。

7. Relative Size Comparison 大小的相对比较

Earth and the Universe 地球与宇宙

Earth is tiny compared with the solar system. The solar system is tiny compared with the Milky Way. The Milky Way is only one galaxy among countless galaxies in the universe.

与太阳系相比，地球非常渺小；与银河系相比，太阳系也很渺小；而银河系只是宇宙中无数星系之一。

Star sizes 恒星大小

Very large stars are called supergiants, large stars are giants, and small stars are dwarfs.

很大的恒星称为超巨星，较大的称为巨星，较小的称为矮星。

9. Final Exam Preparation 期末考试准备

Q1. What is a galaxy? 什么是星系？

English: A galaxy is a huge collection of stars, gas and dust particles held together by gravity.

中文：星系是由引力结合在一起的大量恒星、气体和尘埃粒子所组成的巨大天体系统。

Q2. State the three types of galaxies. 写出三种星系类型。

English: The three types are spiral galaxies, elliptical galaxies and irregular galaxies.

中文：三种星系类型是螺旋星系、椭圆星系和不规则星系。

Q3. Why is the Sun a star? 为什么太阳是一颗恒星？

English: The Sun is a star because it produces its own light and heat.

中文：太阳是一颗恒星，因为它能够自行发光和发热。

Q4. How are stars born? 恒星如何形成？

English: Stars are formed from nebulae. Gas and dust are pulled together by gravitational force, forming a dense core. When hydrogen changes to helium and releases heat and light, a star is formed.

中文：恒星由星云形成。气体和尘埃在引力作用下聚集，形成致密核心。当氢转变成氦并释放热和光时，一颗恒星便形成了。

Q5. How are star colours related to temperature? 恒星颜色与温度有什么关系？

English: Generally, red stars have lower surface temperature while blue stars have higher surface temperature.

中文：一般来说，红色恒星表面温度较低，而蓝色恒星表面温度较高。

Q6. What can a massive star become after a supernova? 大质量恒星在超新星爆炸后会变成什么？

English: It can become a neutron star or a black hole.

中文：它可以变成中子星或黑洞。

Scoring Strategy 得分策略

  • Use keywords: gravity, nebula, spiral galaxy, white dwarf, supernova, black hole.
    善用关键词：引力、星云、螺旋星系、白矮星、超新星、黑洞。
  • Answer directly first, then add one explanation sentence.
    先直接作答，再补上一句解释。
  • For comparison questions, write both the similarity and the difference.
    比较题要写出相同点与不同点。
