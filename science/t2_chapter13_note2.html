1 · Movement of a meteoroid流星体的运行

A meteoroid is a small fragment of rock and metal that floats and moves in outer space. When a meteoroid enters the Earth's atmosphere, it will be called a meteor. A meteor that reaches the Earth is called a meteorite. A crater will form as a result of the meteorite's impact.

流星体是漂浮并在外太空中运行的细小石块与金属碎片。当流星体进入地球的大气层时，它会被称为流星。抵达地球的流星称为陨石。陨石撞击后会形成陨石坑。

2 · Movement of an asteroid小行星的运行

Asteroids move at an average speed of 25 km s⁻¹. Most asteroids are found in the asteroid belt. However, some asteroid orbits lie outside the belt, such as Apollo, Amor and Aten. Collisions with large asteroids can result in the destruction of living things on Earth.

小行星以平均25 km/s的速度运行。大多数小行星位于小行星带内。然而，部分小行星轨道位于带外，例如阿波罗群、阿莫尔群与阿登群。与大型小行星碰撞可能导致地球上生物的毁灭。

3 · Movement of a comet彗星的运行

Comets move at a speed range of 10 to 70 km s⁻¹. Most comets come from the Kuiper belt and Oort cloud. When a comet gets closer to the Sun, it increases in speed, melts, and appears to have a long tail. The tail of a comet always points away from the Sun due to solar wind.

彗星以10至70 km/s的速度范围运行。大多数彗星来自柯伊伯带与奥尔特云。当彗星靠近太阳时，它会加速、融化，并出现长长的尾巴。彗尾因太阳风的吹动而总是背向太阳。

Bahagian C · Q1C部分 · 第1题

Object P in space太空中的物体P

The diagram shows Object P — a tailed object approaching Earth.图中显示物体P——一个带尾巴、正接近地球的天体。

Object P has a glowing head and a bright tail, streaking towards Earth.物体P有发光的头部与明亮的尾巴，正朝地球划过。

(a)
1 mark
What is object P?物体P是什么？
A comet — it has a glowing head and a long tail.彗星——它有发光的头部与长长的尾巴。
(b)
1 mark
Does object P move in its own orbit?物体P是否在自己的轨道上运行？
Yes — a comet moves in its own elliptical orbit around the Sun.是——彗星在自己围绕太阳运行的椭圆轨道上运行。
(c)
1 mark
What influences the movement of object P?是什么影响了物体P的运行？
The gravitational pull of the Sun and outer planets; solar wind also shapes the direction of its tail.太阳与外行星的引力；太阳风也影响其尾巴的方向。
(d)
1 mark
Why does object P's tail form and glow?为什么物体P的尾巴会形成并发光？
As it nears the Sun, heat melts and vaporises its ice and dust, releasing gas that glows and streams away from the Sun.当它靠近太阳时，热量使其冰与尘埃融化并汽化，释放出会发光并朝远离太阳方向流出的气体。
(e)
1 mark
State one difference between object P and an asteroid.说出物体P与小行星之间的一个差别。
Object P (a comet) is made of ice, gas and frozen dust and has a tail; an asteroid is made of rock and metal and has no tail.物体P（彗星）由冰、气体与冻结尘埃组成，并有尾巴；小行星则由岩石与金属组成，没有尾巴。
Bahagian C · Q1(f)C部分 · 第1(f)题

Reading: the Apophis asteroid阅读理解：阿波菲斯小行星

"In 2013, NASA stated the probability of the Apophis asteroid colliding with the Earth in 2036." 「2013年，NASA表示阿波菲斯小行星在2036年撞击地球的可能性。」
(i)
1 mark
What kind of scale is used to measure how much an asteroid impact could endanger Earth?用什么样的量表来衡量小行星撞击对地球的危害程度？
A numbered hazard scale (such as the Torino Scale) that rates the risk from 0 — no hazard — up to 10 — a certain, globally catastrophic collision. (Check this against your teacher's answer scheme, as the exact scale name isn't printed on the page you shared.)一种以数字分级的危险量表（例如托里诺量表），从0（无危险）到10（确定发生、造成全球性灾难的撞击）。（由于你提供的页面上没有印出确切的量表名称，请以老师的答案为准。）
(ii)
1 mark
Explain one step used to prevent an asteroid impact.解释一个用来防止小行星撞击的方法。
Scientists can send a spacecraft to nudge or deflect the asteroid so it changes course and misses Earth, or attach explosives to break it into smaller, safer fragments.科学家可派遣太空船轻推或使小行星偏转，使其改变路线而避开地球，或在其上安装炸药使其碎裂成较小、较安全的碎片。
Modul PBD · HOTSPBD模组 · 高阶思维题

Predict & suggest预测与建议

4
Predict the condition of Earth if a meteoroid, asteroid or comet collides with it.预测流星体、小行星或彗星撞击地球后的情况。
A large impact could destroy buildings and living things near the impact zone, throw dust into the atmosphere that blocks sunlight and changes temperatures, and — if the object is very large (≥10 km) — even cause mass extinction, as scientists believe happened to the dinosaurs.大规模撞击可能摧毁撞击区附近的建筑物与生物，将尘埃扬入大气层遮挡阳光并改变气温，若撞击物极大（≥10公里），甚至可能如科学家所推测导致恐龙灭绝般，造成物种大灭绝。
5
Suggest two methods scientists use to avoid an asteroid collision.建议两种科学家用来避免小行星撞击的方法。
(1) Change the asteroid's course using a spacecraft. (2) Break it apart with explosives or a bomb before it reaches Earth.（1）利用太空船改变小行星的运行路线。（2）在小行星抵达地球前用炸药或炸弹将其炸碎。
