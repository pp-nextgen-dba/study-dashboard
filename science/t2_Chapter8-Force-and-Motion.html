Force and Motion 力与运动

From a pushed trolley to a sunken stone, every force has a reaction, every lever has a balance point, and every gas pushes back. This chapter is about how pushes, pulls, weight, and pressure quietly run the physical world. 从被推动的手推车到沉入水中的石头，每一个力都有反作用力，每一个杠杆都有平衡点，每一种气体都会反推。本章探讨推力、拉力、重量与压力如何悄悄主宰物理世界。

8.1

Force 力

What is force? 什么是力？

A force is a pull or a push acting upon an object. We cannot see a force but we can feel or observe its effects.

力是作用在物体上的拉力或推力。我们看不见力，但能感觉到或观察到它的效果。

S.I. unit of force = newton (N) 力的国际单位是牛顿（N）· 1 kg ≈ 10 N（地球重力近似值）

Measured using a spring balance — the heavier the load, the more the spring extends, the bigger the reading.

用弹簧测力计测量——负载越重，弹簧伸展越多，读数越大。

Characteristics of force 力的特性

Force is a vector quantity with three characteristics:

  • Magnitude — the strength of the force 大小 — 力的强度
  • Direction — the path the force moves toward 方向 — 力作用的路径
  • Point of application — where the force is applied 作用点 — 力施加的位置

Types of forces 力的种类

  • Gravitational force — pulls objects to Earth's centre 重力 — 把物体拉向地心
  • Normal force — perpendicular to a contact surface 正向力 — 垂直于接触面
  • Frictional force — resists movement between surfaces 摩擦力 — 阻碍接触面间的运动

More force types 更多力的种类

  • Weight — gravitational force acting on an object 重量 — 作用在物体上的重力
  • Elastic force — when a spring is stretched/compressed 弹力 — 弹簧被拉伸或压缩时产生
  • Buoyant force — resists the weight of an object in air/liquid 浮力 — 抵抗物体在空气或液体中的重量

Tension force 张力

A force acting on an object that is suspended on a rope. The arrowhead shows the direction the force acts in.

作用在悬挂于绳子上的物体的力。箭头表示力的作用方向。

8.1b

Action–Reaction Pairs 作用力与反作用力

Newton's Third Law 牛顿第三运动定律

For every action force, there is a reaction force of the same magnitude but in the opposite direction.

每一个作用力都有一个大小相同、方向相反的反作用力。

  • Book on a table: weight (action, ↓) = normal force (reaction, ↑) 桌上的书：重量（作用力，向下）= 正向力（反作用力，向上）
  • Swimmer floating: weight (action, ↓) = buoyant force (reaction, ↑) 漂浮的游泳者：重量（作用力，向下）= 浮力（反作用力，向上）

More examples 更多例子

  • Two trolleys + compressed spring: released, they move in opposite directions over the same distance 两台手推车 + 压缩弹簧：释放后向相反方向移动相同距离
  • Pushing a box: woman pushes box (action) → box pushes back on woman (reaction) 推箱子：女子推箱子（作用力）→ 箱子反推女子（反作用力）
  • Rowing a boat: paddle pushes water back → water pushes boat forward 划船：船桨向后推水 → 水向前推船
8.2

Effects of Force & Buoyancy 力的效应与浮力

What force can change 力能改变什么

When a force acts on an object, it can change the object's size, shape, and motion.

力作用于物体时，能改变物体的大小、形状和运动状态。

  • Pushing a stationary object → it moves in the direction of the force 推动静止物体 → 物体沿力的方向移动
  • Force in opposite direction → object stops moving 反方向施力 → 物体停止移动
  • Force at an angle to a moving object → direction of motion changes 对运动物体斜向施力 → 运动方向改变
  • Pressing from two opposite directions → shape and size change 从两个相反方向挤压 → 形状和大小改变

Buoyant force 浮力

Buoyancy in water depends on the magnitude of weight (action force, ↓) and buoyant force (reaction force, ↑).

浮力取决于重量（作用力，向下）和浮力（反作用力，向上）的大小。

Buoyant force = Actual weight − Apparent weight 实际重量 = 物体在空气中的重量 · 表观重量 = 物体浸入水中的重量
Condition 条件Result 结果
F > W 浮力 > 重量Object floats (less dense than water) 物体漂浮（密度小于水）
F = W 浮力 = 重量Object suspended at a depth (same density) 物体悬浮于某深度（密度相同）
F < W 浮力 < 重量Object sinks (denser than water) 物体下沉（密度大于水）

Plimsoll lines 船舶载重线

Lines marked on a ship's body to indicate the maximum safe depth it can be immersed when loaded, since seawater density varies with temperature and salt content in different parts of the world.

标记在船身上的线，用以表示船舶载货时可安全浸入的最大深度，因为不同地区海水的密度会因温度和盐含量而不同。

8.1c

Moment of Force & Levers 力矩与杠杆

Moment of force 力矩

The turning effect produced when a force acts on an object at a fixed point (pivot/fulcrum).

当力作用在固定点（支点/转轴）上的物体时所产生的转动效应。

Moment of force = Force (N) × perpendicular distance from pivot (m) 力矩 = 力（牛顿）× 力到支点的垂直距离（米）

Moment increases by: (a) increasing the force applied, or (b) moving the force further from the pivot.

增大力矩的方法：(a) 增加施加的力，或 (b) 把力移到离支点更远处。

Principle of moments 力矩原理

Anticlockwise moment = Clockwise moment 逆时针力矩 = 顺时针力矩 → 杠杆系统达到平衡

Load (N) × distance of load from fulcrum (m) = Effort (N) × distance of effort from fulcrum (m)

负荷（N）× 负荷到支点的距离（m）= 用力（N）× 用力到支点的距离（m）

Worked example — see-saw 例题 — 跷跷板

X (20 kg) sits at distance d; Y (10 kg) sits 2 m from fulcrum. Balanced see-saw. > 20 × 10 N × d = 10 × 10 N × 2 m > d = 200 N·m / 200 N = 1 m

First class lever 第一类杠杆

  • Fulcrum is between load and effort 支点位于负荷与用力之间
  • Load and effort act in same direction 负荷与用力方向相同
  • e.g. scissors, pliers, see-saw 例：剪刀、钳子、跷跷板

Second class lever 第二类杠杆

  • Load is between fulcrum and effort 负荷位于支点与用力之间
  • Small effort overcomes a heavy load 小用力可克服重负荷
  • e.g. wheelbarrow, nutcracker, bottle opener 例：手推车、坚果夹、开瓶器

Third class lever 第三类杠杆

  • Effort is between fulcrum and load 用力位于支点与负荷之间
  • Large effort needed; effort must be near load 需要较大用力；用力点须靠近负荷
  • e.g. fishing rod, broom, human arm, tongs 例：钓鱼竿、扫帚、人的手臂、夹子
8.3a

Pressure 压强

Definition & formula 定义与公式

Pressure is the normal force acting perpendicularly on a surface per unit area.

压强是垂直作用于物体表面单位面积上的正向力。

Pressure (N m⁻²) = Normal force (N) / Surface area (m²) 单位：牛顿每平方米（N m⁻²）或帕斯卡（Pa）· 1 N m⁻² = 1 Pa

The bigger the surface area, the smaller the pressure produced for the same force.

同样的力，表面积越大，产生的压强越小。

Applications 日常应用

Increasing pressure (smaller area): knife blades, thumbtacks, football boot studs, ice skate blades.

增加压强（减小面积）：刀刃、图钉、足球鞋鞴钉、滑冰鞋刀片。

Decreasing pressure (larger area): shoulder pads, snowboards, tractor tyres.

减少压强（增大面积）：肩垫、滑雪板、拖拉机轮胎。

Worked example 例题

Force = 10 N, nail end cross-section = 0.01 cm² > Pressure = 10 / 0.01 = 1000 N cm⁻² = 1.0 × 10⁷ N m⁻²
8.3b

Gas & Atmospheric Pressure 气压与大气压

Kinetic theory of gases 气体分子运动论

Air is made of tiny gas particles (molecules) moving randomly at high speed. They collide with container walls — this force per area is air pressure.

空气由微小的气体粒子（分子）组成，以高速随机运动。粒子撞击容器壁的力即为气压。

Factor 因素Effect on pressure 对压强的影响
Volume ↓ 体积减小Pressure ↑ (more collisions) 压强增加（碰撞更频繁）
Volume ↑ 体积增大Pressure ↓ 压强减小
Temperature ↑ 温度升高Particles move faster → Pressure ↑ 粒子移动更快 → 压强增加
Temperature ↓ 温度降低Particles move slower → Pressure ↓ 粒子移动更慢 → 压强减小

Atmospheric pressure & altitude 大气压与高度

The atmosphere exerts pressure on everything on Earth's surface — this is atmospheric pressure.

大气对地球表面所有物体施加压力——这就是大气压。

As altitude increases, atmospheric pressure decreases (air is thinner, fewer particle collisions).

随着高度增加，大气压减小（空气更稀薄，粒子碰撞较少）。

Daily-life applications 日常应用

  • Plunger — reduced pressure clears blockages 吸盘 — 降低压强以疏通堵塞物
  • Drinking straw — lower pressure inside draws liquid up 吸管 — 内部压强降低，吸起液体
  • Syphon — transfers liquid from high to low level 虹吸管 — 把液体从高处转移到低处
  • Vacuum cleaner — low pressure sucks in dust 真空吸尘器 — 低压吸入灰尘
  • Rubber sucker hook — atmospheric pressure holds it on the wall 吸盘挂钩 — 大气压使其吸附在墙上
  • Condensed milk can — needs 2 holes for air pressure balance 炼乳罐 — 需要两个孔以平衡气压
8.3c

Liquid Pressure 液体压强

Effects of depth 深度的影响

Liquid pressure is caused by the weight of the liquid acting on a body. It increases with the depth of the liquid.

液体压强由作用在物体上的液体重量引起。它随液体的深度增加而增大。

  • Deeper hole → liquid jet squirts further/stronger 孔越深 → 液体喷射得更远/更强
  • Air bubbles get bigger as they rise (pressure decreases towards surface) 气泡上升时变大（压强随接近水面而减小）

Daily-life applications 日常应用

  • Submarines — thick steel/titanium cylindrical body withstands deep-ocean pressure 潜水艇 — 厚钢/钛圆柱体身能承受深海压力
  • Diving suits — strong, rigid materials withstand high water pressure 潜水服 — 坚固刚性材料能承受高水压
  • Dam walls — built thicker and curved at the base to spread pressure evenly 水坝壁 — 底部加厚并呈弧形以均匀分散压力
