3. Key Terms / 重点术语

↔️

Force / 力

Push or pull on an object.
对物体的推或拉。

🌍

Gravitational force / 重力

Force caused by gravity pulling an object.
由地球引力造成的力。

🌀

Elastic force / 弹力

Force when a material is stretched or compressed.
物体被拉长或压缩时产生的力。

⚖️

Weight / 重量

Force acting on a mass due to gravity.
重力作用在物体上的力。

👟

Frictional force / 摩擦力

Force between two surfaces in contact, opposing motion.
两个接触面之间阻碍运动的力。

📏

Magnitude / 大小

The amount or size of a force.
力的数值或大小。

⬆️

Normal force / 支持力

The force produced by a surface in contact with an object.
表面对接触物体产生的支持力。

🚪

Point of application / 作用点

The exact place where the force acts.
力施加的准确位置。

4. Types of Forces / 力的种类

👟

Frictional force / 摩擦力

Acts between two touching surfaces.

存在于两个接触面之间。

Example: Shoes grip the ground when walking.
例子：走路时鞋子与地面之间有摩擦力。
🚢

Buoyant force / 浮力

Upward force exerted by a fluid.

液体或气体向上托起物体的力。

Example: A boat floats on water.
例子：船浮在水面上。
🍎

Gravitational force / 重力

Pulls objects toward Earth.

把物体拉向地球。

Example: An apple falls down.
例子：苹果往下掉。
🪀

Elastic force / 弹力

Exists when a spring or elastic object is stretched or compressed.

弹簧或弹性物体被拉长或压缩时产生的力。

Example: A stretched spring pulls back.
例子：被拉长的弹簧会拉回去。
📚

Normal force / 支持力

Support force from a surface.

表面对物体提供的支持力。

Example: A table supports a book.
例子：桌子支撑着书本。
🎒

Weight / 重量

Force due to gravity acting on an object.

重力作用在物体上的力。

Example: A school bag has weight.
例子：书包有重量。

5. Characteristics of Force / 力的特征

1. Magnitude / 大小

The amount of force, such as 10 N or 500 N.

力的大小，例如 10 N 或 500 N。

2. Direction / 方向

The direction in which the force acts: up, down, left, right, forward, backward.

力作用的方向：上、下、左、右、向前、向后。

3. Point of Application / 作用点

The exact point where the force is applied, such as the hand pushing a car.

力施加的准确位置，例如手推车的地方。

Force is a vector quantity. It has magnitude and direction.
力是一个矢量，它有大小和方向。

6. How to Measure Force / 如何测量力

05101520 N

Tool: Spring balance
工具：弹簧秤

Unit: newton (N)
单位：牛顿（N）

Read the value shown on the scale of the spring balance.

读取弹簧秤刻度上显示的数值。

Remember: meter ruler measures length, but spring balance measures force.
记住：米尺测量长度，弹簧秤测量力。

7. Newton’s Third Law / 牛顿第三定律

For every action force, there is a reaction force of the same magnitude but in the opposite direction.
每一个作用力，都有一个大小相同但方向相反的反作用力。
📘⬆️⬇️

Book on a table / 书本放在桌上

Weight pulls the book downward. The table pushes the book upward with normal force.

重量把书往下拉，桌子用支持力把书向上推。

🏊‍♂️↔️🧱

Swimmer pushes wall / 游泳者推墙

The swimmer pushes the wall backward, and the wall pushes the swimmer forward.

游泳者向后推墙，墙也向前推游泳者。

🦶⚽

Kicking a ball / 踢球

The foot pushes the ball forward, and the ball pushes the foot backward.

脚把球向前踢，球也向后推脚。

8. Worked Examples / 例题讲解

Easy / 简单

Spring with loads / 弹簧与重物

Question: Which force acts on the spring when it is stretched?

问题：当弹簧被拉长时，作用在弹簧上的是什么力？

Answer: Elastic force / 答案：弹力
Medium / 中等

Cyclist / 骑自行车

Question: A force of 10 N is shown. State the magnitude and direction.

问题：图中显示 10 N 的力，请写出它的大小和方向。

Answer: Magnitude = 10 N, Direction = backward.
答案：大小 = 10 N，方向 = 向后。
Hard / 较难

Book on table / 书在桌上

Question: According to Newton’s Third Law, what happens?

问题：根据牛顿第三定律，会发生什么？

Answer: Normal force and weight act in opposite directions.
答案：支持力与重量方向相反。

9. Quick Practice / 快速练习

1

Which force keeps you from slipping while walking?

走路时，哪一种力防止你滑倒？

2

A ball is thrown upward. Which force pulls it down?

球被抛向上方后，哪一种力把它拉下来？

3

A spring is stretched. What force acts in the spring?

弹簧被拉长时，弹簧里存在什么力？

4

Which force acts perpendicular to the surface of a table?

哪一种力垂直作用在桌面上？

5

If you push a wall, what will the wall do to you?

如果你推墙，墙会对你做什么？

Show Answers / 显示答案
  1. Frictional force / 摩擦力
  2. Gravitational force / 重力
  3. Elastic force / 弹力
  4. Normal force / 支持力
  5. The wall pushes you back with an equal force in the opposite direction. / 墙会以大小相同、方向相反的力推回你。

10. Parent Guide / 家长讲解小贴士

How to explain “force” / 怎样解释“力”

Use daily examples: pushing a door, pulling a drawer, kicking a ball.

用生活例子来说明：推门、拉抽屉、踢球。

Ask 3 simple questions / 记住 3 个问题

How big? Which direction? Where is the force applied?

多大？什么方向？力作用在哪里？

For exam answers / 回答考试题时

Encourage your son to use key words: magnitude, direction, point of application, equal force, opposite direction.

鼓励孩子使用关键词：大小、方向、作用点、大小相同、方向相反。

Best study order / 最佳学习顺序

Memory map → key terms → examples → practice questions.

记忆导图 → 重点术语 → 例题 → 练习题。

11. Exam Tips / 考试技巧

📌

Remember the unit / 记住单位

Force is measured in newton (N).
力的单位是牛顿（N）。

➡️

Show direction / 写清方向

Up, down, left, right, forward or backward.
上、下、左、右、前、后都要写清楚。

🎯

State the point / 写出作用点

For example: hand, foot, table, wall.
例如：手、脚、桌子、墙。

🔁

Newton’s Law / 牛顿第三定律

Always mention “same magnitude, opposite direction”.
一定写“大小相同，方向相反”。

Study smart: Review the memory map first, then practise the questions.
先复习记忆导图，再练习题目，学习效果会更好。
