2 What is Force? / 什么是力？

Simple idea / 简单理解

Force is a push or pull on an object.
力是作用在物体上的推力或拉力。

Force is a vector quantity, so it has magnitude and direction.
力是矢量，具有大小和方向。

Unit: N1 kg ≈ 10 N on Earth100 g ≈ 1 N

3 Types of Forces / 力的种类

🌍
Gravitational force / 重力
Attraction towards Earth. / 地球对物体的吸引力。
⚖️
Weight / 重量
Gravitational force acting on mass. / 重力作用在物体上的力。
⬆️
Normal force / 支持力
Support force from a surface. / 表面对物体的支撑力。
🧱
Frictional force / 摩擦力
Opposes motion between touching surfaces. / 阻碍接触表面相对运动。
〰️
Elastic force / 弹力
Helps object return to original shape. / 使物体恢复原状。
🛟
Buoyant force / 浮力
Upward force from fluid. / 液体向上的力。

4 Effects of Force / 力的作用

  • Moves a stationary object / 使静止物体移动
  • Stops a moving object / 使运动物体停止
  • Changes direction / 改变运动方向
  • Changes speed / 改变速度
  • Changes shape or size / 改变形状或大小
Exam phrase: “A force can change the state of motion or shape of an object.”
考试句子：力可以改变物体的运动状态或形状。

5 Newton’s Third Law / 牛顿第三定律

For every action, there is an equal reaction in the opposite direction.每一个作用力，都有一个大小相等、方向相反的反作用力。
🍎
Apple on table
苹果压桌子，桌子向上支持苹果。
🚣
Rowing boat
桨向后推水，水向前推船。
🚗
Trolley + spring
弹簧互推，两车反方向移动。
🦆
Floating duck
重量向下，浮力向上。

6 Buoyant Force & Density / 浮力与密度

Floating rule / 浮沉规则
Object floats when buoyant force can support its weight.
当浮力足以支撑重量时，物体会浮起来。
Buoyant force = Real weight − Apparent weight浮力 = 真实重量 − 视重量
Density = Mass ÷ Volume密度 = 质量 ÷ 体积
Comparison / 比较Result / 结果
Density of object < density of water
物体密度 < 水密度		Floats / 浮起
Density of object > density of water
物体密度 > 水密度		Sinks / 下沉

7 Lever / 杠杆

A lever is a simple machine that can rotate around a fixed point. It helps us do work using less force.
杠杆是能绕固定点转动的简单机械，可帮助我们省力。

First class lever / 第一类杠杆

EL

Fulcrum between effort and load.
支点在用力和负载之间。

Examples: scissors, pliers / 剪刀、钳子

Second class lever / 第二类杠杆

EL

Load between fulcrum and effort.
负载在支点和用力之间。

Examples: nutcracker, wheelbarrow / 核桃夹、独轮车

Third class lever / 第三类杠杆

LE

Effort between fulcrum and load.
用力在支点和负载之间。

Examples: ice tongs, fishing rod / 冰夹、钓鱼竿

8 Moment of Force / 力矩

Moment of force is the turning effect produced when a force acts on an object at a fixed point.
力矩是力使物体绕固定点转动所产生的转动效果。

Moment of force = Force × perpendicular distance力矩 = 力 × 到支点的垂直距离
Important: Longer perpendicular distance gives larger moment, even if the force is the same.
重点：力相同，垂直距离越长，力矩越大。
Balance: Load × distance = Force × distance平衡：负载 × 距离 = 用力 × 距离

9 Pressure / 压强

Pressure = Force ÷ Surface area压强 = 力 ÷ 受力面积

SI unit: Pascal, Pa1 Pa = 1 N/m²

Situation / 情况Pressure / 压强Example / 例子
Large surface area / 面积大Low / 低Tractor tyres, snow shoes / 拖拉机轮、雪鞋
Small surface area / 面积小High / 高Knife blade, skating boots, football studs / 刀刃、冰鞋、鞋钉

10 Gas Pressure & Atmospheric Pressure / 气压与大气压

Gas pressure / 气体压强

Air molecules move randomly and collide with the wall of the container. The force on the wall produces gas pressure.
空气分子随机运动并撞击容器壁，对壁产生的力形成气体压强。

  • Temperature increases → particles move faster → pressure increases.
    温度上升 → 分子运动更快 → 压强增加。
  • Volume decreases → more collisions → pressure increases.
    体积减少 → 碰撞更频繁 → 压强增加。

Atmospheric pressure / 大气压

Atmospheric pressure is pressure exerted by air on Earth’s surface.
大气压是空气对地球表面施加的压强。

Higher altitude → lower atmospheric pressure.
海拔越高，大气压越低。

11 Air Pressure in Daily Life / 气压应用

🪠
Plunger / 吸盘
Low pressure inside, higher atmospheric pressure outside pushes it firmly. / 内部低压，外部较高大气压把它压紧。
🥤
Straw / 吸管
Lower pressure inside straw allows atmospheric pressure to push drink up. / 吸管内气压低，外部大气压把饮料推上来。
💉
Syringe / 注射器
Pull plunger → volume increases → pressure decreases → water enters. / 拉活塞使体积增加，压强降低，水被推入。
🧹
Vacuum cleaner / 吸尘器
Creates low pressure; outside air pushes dust in. / 制造低压，外部空气把灰尘推入。

12 Pressure in Liquid / 液体压强

Core rule / 核心规律

Liquid pressure increases as depth increases.
深度越深，液体压强越大。

Why? / 为什么？

At greater depth, there is more liquid above the object. The weight of liquid above causes greater pressure.
深处上方液体更多，液体重量更大，因此压强更大。

Examples / 例子

  • Dam wall is wider at the bottom / 大坝底部更宽
  • Diver suit resists high pressure / 潜水服抗高压
  • Submarine body is strong / 潜水艇外壳坚固
  • Air bubbles expand as they rise / 气泡上升时变大

13 Exam Quick Tips / 考试速记

✅ Identify the type of force first. / 先判断是哪一种力。
✅ For floating/sinking, compare density with water. / 判断浮沉时，比较物体密度和水的密度。
✅ Formula questions: write formula → substitute values → answer with unit. / 计算题：写公式 → 代入数值 → 写单位。
✅ Newton’s Third Law answers must mention “same magnitude” and “opposite direction”. / 牛顿第三定律答案要写“大小相等、方向相反”。
✅ Lever questions: label fulcrum, load, effort and distance. / 杠杆题要标出支点、负载、用力和距离。

14 Mini Quiz / 小测验

Why does a ship float on water?
船为什么能浮在水面上？
Hint: buoyant force supports weight. / 提示：浮力支撑重量。
Which class of lever is a wheelbarrow?
独轮车属于哪一类杠杆？
Answer: second class lever. / 第二类杠杆。
What happens to atmospheric pressure when altitude increases?
海拔升高时，大气压怎样变化？
Answer: it decreases. / 大气压降低。
Why do tractor tyres sink less into soft soil?
为什么拖拉机轮胎不容易陷入软土？
Hint: larger surface area gives lower pressure. / 面积大，压强小。
An object has density 1.3 g/cm³ in water. Sink or float?
物体密度 1.3 g/cm³，在水中下沉还是上浮？
Answer: sink, because 1.3 > 1.0 g/cm³. / 下沉，因为密度比水大。
⭐ Key Idea / 核心思想：Force explains how objects move, stop, change direction, change shape, float, sink and exert pressure. 力帮助我们解释物体如何运动、停止、改变方向、变形、浮沉和产生压强。
