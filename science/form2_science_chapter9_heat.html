1. Heat vs Temperature
热 与 温度

Heat: energy, unit J. Temperature: degree of hotness/coldness, unit °C or K. 热：能量，单位 J。温度：冷热程度，单位 °C 或 K。

2. Heat Transfer
热传递

Conduction, convection, radiation. 传导、对流、辐射。

3. Conductors & Insulators
导热体与隔热体

Metals conduct heat fast. Air, plastic, cotton, wool and polystyrene insulate. 金属导热快；空气、塑料、棉、羊毛、聚苯乙烯隔热。

4. Expansion & Contraction
膨胀与收缩

Matter expands when heated and contracts when cooled. 物质受热膨胀，冷却收缩。

5. Surface Type & Colour
表面种类与颜色

Dull/dark surfaces absorb and radiate heat better. Shiny/white surfaces reflect heat better. 暗色粗糙表面吸热、放热较好；白色光亮表面反射热较好。

6. Daily Life Applications
生活应用

Thermostat, thermometer, fire alarm, railway gaps, loose cables, thermos flask, green building. 恒温器、温度计、火警、铁路缝隙、松弛电缆、保温瓶、绿色建筑。

9.1 Relationship between Temperature and Heat / 温度与热的关系

Heat / 热

Heat is a form of energy that flows from a hotter region to a colder region.

热是一种能量，会从较热的区域流向较冷的区域。

Unit 单位: joule, J / 焦耳 J

Temperature / 温度

Temperature is the measurement of the degree of hotness or coldness of an object.

温度是物体冷热程度的测量。

Instrument 仪器: thermometer / 温度计
Units 单位: °C or K

9.2 Heat Flow and Thermal Equilibrium / 热流动与热平衡

Heat flow / 热流动

Heat flows from higher temperature to lower temperature. 热从高温流向低温。

Thermal equilibrium / 热平衡

Reached when two objects in contact have the same temperature. No net heat transfer. 两个接触物体温度相同时达到热平衡；没有净热转移。

Rate of heat flow / 热流动速率

The greater the temperature difference, the faster heat flows. 温度差越大，热流动越快。

Daytime 白天: Land heats faster. Warm air over land rises. Cool air from sea moves to land. Sea → Land.
陆地升温较快，陆地上方暖空气上升，海上冷空气吹向陆地。海 → 陆。

Night 夜晚: Land cools faster. Warm air over sea rises. Cool air from land moves to sea. Land → Sea.
陆地冷却较快，海上暖空气上升，陆地冷空气吹向海洋。陆 → 海。

Daily-life uses / 生活用途

Conductors 导热体

  • Cooking pots and pans transfer heat quickly to food. 锅和平底锅快速把热传给食物。
  • Soldering iron tip is copper. 焊铁尖端用铜。
  • Iron base conducts heat to clothes. 熨斗底部导热给衣服。
  • Refrigerator copper pipes conduct heat away. 冰箱铜管把热带走。
  • Motorcycle fins conduct heat away from engine. 摩托车散热片把引擎热散走。

Insulators 隔热体

  • Plastic/wood handles prevent burns. 塑料/木柄防烫。
  • Cotton and wool trap air to keep warm. 棉和羊毛困住空气来保暖。
  • Oven and refrigerator walls reduce heat transfer. 烤箱和冰箱墙壁减少热传递。
  • Sawdust slows melting of ice. 木屑减慢冰融化。
  • Polystyrene cup is less hot to hold. 聚苯乙烯杯较不烫手。

9.3 Expansion and Contraction of Matter / 物质的膨胀与收缩

When heated / 受热时

Particles vibrate or move faster, become further apart, volume increases, object expands. 粒子振动/移动更快，距离变大，体积增加，物体膨胀。

When cooled / 冷却时

Particles vibrate or move slower, become closer together, volume decreases, object contracts. 粒子振动/移动变慢，距离变小，体积减少，物体收缩。

Examples in solids, liquids and gases / 固体、液体、气体例子

Matter 物质Heated 受热Cooled 冷却
Solid 固体Metal ball expands and cannot pass through ring.
金属球膨胀，不能穿过金属环。		Metal ball contracts and can pass through ring.
金属球收缩，可以穿过金属环。
Liquid 液体Coloured water expands; level rises.
有色水膨胀，水位上升。		Coloured water contracts; level drops.
有色水收缩，水位下降。
Gas 气体Air expands; liquid level rises.
空气膨胀，液位上升。		Air contracts; liquid level drops.
空气收缩，液位下降。

Uses of expansion and contraction / 膨胀与收缩的用途

Thermometer 温度计

Mercury expands when temperature rises and contracts when temperature falls. 温度上升时水银膨胀；温度下降时水银收缩。

Bimetallic thermometer 双金属温度计

Two metals expand at different rates; strip bends and moves the pointer. 两种金属膨胀速率不同，金属片弯曲并推动指针。

Thermostat 恒温器

Bimetallic strip acts as an automatic switch to maintain stable temperature. 双金属片作为自动开关，维持稳定温度。

Electric iron 电熨斗

When too hot, bimetallic strip bends away from contact screw and cuts off current. When cooled, it returns and completes circuit. 太热时双金属片弯离接触螺丝，切断电流；冷却后回位，电路接通。

Fire alarm 火警钟

Heat bends the bimetallic strip to touch the contact point, completing the circuit and ringing the alarm. 热使双金属片弯曲接触接点，完成电路，使警钟响起。

Wheel and axle 轮与轴

Axle is cooled to contract, inserted into hub, then expands at room temperature to fit tightly. 轴冷却后收缩，插入轮毂，回到室温后膨胀并紧密固定。

Rivet 铆钉

Hot rivet expands. After hammering and cooling, it contracts and holds metal pieces tightly. 热铆钉膨胀；敲打后冷却收缩，把金属片紧紧固定。

Railway tracks 铁轨

Gaps allow rails to expand in hot weather and prevent buckling. 铁轨间隙让铁轨在热天膨胀，避免弯曲变形。

Cables 电缆

Cables are hung loosely. They expand and become slack in daytime; contract and become taut at night. 电缆要松挂；白天膨胀变松，夜晚收缩变紧。

Steel bridges 钢桥

One end fixed, the other supported by rollers to allow expansion and contraction. 一端固定，另一端有滚轮，让桥梁膨胀和收缩。

Concrete roads 混凝土路

Gaps allow expansion and prevent cracking. 缝隙允许膨胀，防止道路裂开。

Hot water pipes 热水管

Coiled pipes or curved connectors allow expansion and contraction, preventing damage. 盘绕或弯曲接头允许膨胀收缩，防止损坏。

Dented table tennis ball 乒乓球凹陷

Hot water heats the air inside; air expands and restores shape. 热水加热球内空气，空气膨胀，使乒乓球恢复形状。

Tight metal lid 太紧的金属瓶盖

Hot water makes the metal lid expand, so it becomes easier to open. 热水使金属盖膨胀，较容易打开。

9.4 Surfaces, Heat Absorption and Emission / 表面、吸热与放热

Dull and dark surfaces
暗色、粗糙表面

Absorb heat faster and radiate heat faster. 吸热较快，也放热/辐射热较快。

Bright, shiny and white surfaces
光亮、白色表面

Absorb and radiate heat more slowly; good reflectors of heat. 吸热和放热较慢；是良好的热反射体。

Temperature factor 温度因素

The hotter the object, the more heat it radiates. When an object radiates heat, its temperature drops. 物体越热，辐射的热越多；物体辐射热时，温度会下降。

Daily Life Heat Concepts in Buildings / 建筑物中的热概念

Comfortable temperature / 舒适温度

  • Cool air enters through windows/openings near the ground. 冷空气从靠近地面的窗户或开口进入。
  • Hot air rises and exits through ventilation holes above. 热空气上升并从上方通风孔排出。
  • Ceiling tiles with air bubbles reduce heat entry. 有气泡的天花板砖减少热进入。
  • Concrete walls and gaps between bricks reduce heat transfer. 混凝土墙和砖缝减少热传递。
  • Double-glazed windows trap air as insulation. 双层玻璃窗困住空气作为隔热层。

Heater and air conditioner / 暖气与冷气

Heaters are placed on the floor because hot air rises and circulates. 暖气放在地面，因为热空气会上升并循环。

Air conditioners are placed near the ceiling because cool air sinks. 冷气机安装在靠近天花板，因为冷空气会下沉。

Features 特点Materials/Designs 材料/设计
Efficient use of energy, water and resources; sustainable and eco-friendly; use recycled materials; innovative design; safe internal and external environment.
高效使用能源、水和资源；可持续且环保；使用再循环材料；创新设计；提供安全环境。		Rainwater storage tanks, light sensors, double-glazed glass, heat reflector paint, natural lighting, photovoltaics.

Practice Q&A / 练习问答

Q1. Why are saucepan handles made of plastic? / 为什么锅柄用塑料制成？
Plastic is a poor conductor of heat, so less heat is transferred to the hand and burns are prevented. 塑料是不良导热体，因此较少热传到手上，可避免烫伤。
Q2. Why are railway tracks built with gaps? / 为什么铁轨之间要留缝隙？
The gaps allow the rails to expand in hot weather and prevent buckling. 缝隙让铁轨在热天膨胀，防止弯曲变形。
Q3. What happens if electric cables are installed too taut on a cold night? / 如果电缆安装得太紧，寒冷夜晚会怎样？
The cables contract and become more taut. They may snap or disconnect. 电缆会收缩并变得更紧，可能断裂或脱落。
Q4. How does a thermostat in an electric iron work? / 电熨斗中的恒温器如何工作？
When too hot, the bimetallic strip bends away from the contact screw and cuts off the circuit. When cooled, it returns to its original position and completes the circuit. 太热时，双金属片弯离接触螺丝并切断电路；冷却后回到原位并接通电路。
Q5. Why is a black flask a better heat absorber and radiator than a white flask? / 为什么黑色烧瓶比白色烧瓶更会吸热和放热？
A dull and dark surface absorbs and radiates heat faster than a white and shiny surface. 暗色粗糙表面比白色光亮表面更快吸收和辐射热。
Q6. Why is the air conditioner placed near the ceiling? / 为什么冷气机安装在靠近天花板？
Cool air is denser and flows downwards, so the room is cooled more effectively. 冷空气密度较大，会向下流动，因此房间能更有效冷却。
