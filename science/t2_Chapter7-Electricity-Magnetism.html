7.1

Energy & Its Sources 能量与能量来源

What is energy? 什么是能量？

Energy is the ability to do work. When work is done, energy transfers from one object to another. It's measured in the S.I. unit joule (J).

能量是做功的能力。当功被完成时，能量会从一个物体转移到另一个物体。能量的国际单位是焦耳（J）。

Energy cannot be created or destroyed — only changed in form 能量守恒定律：能量不能被创造或毁灭，只能从一种形式转化为另一种形式

Example — burning petrol: Chemical energy → Heat energy + Light energy

9 forms of energy 九种能量形式

  • Gravitational potential — stored due to position 重力势能 — 因位置而储存
  • Elastic potential — stored due to condition (stretched/compressed) 弹性势能 — 因状态（拉伸/压缩）而储存
  • Kinetic — possessed by a moving object 动能 — 运动物体所拥有
  • Light, Nuclear, Chemical, Heat, Sound, Electrical 光能、核能、化学能、热能、声能、电能

Sources of energy 能量来源

Wave, hydro, solar, fossil fuels, wind, geothermal, biomass, radioactive substances. The Sun is the main source on Earth.

波浪能、水力能、太阳能、化石燃料、风能、地热能、生物质能、放射性物质。太阳是地球上主要的能量来源。

Electrostatic charges 静电荷

Produced when two objects are rubbed together — electrons transfer between them.

当两个物体相互摩擦时产生 — 电子在两者之间转移。

Neutral object 中性物体

Has equal protons & electrons. Loses electrons → positive. Gains electrons → negative.

质子与电子数量相等。失去电子 → 带正电；获得电子 → 带负电。

7.1b

Electrostatic Charges 静电荷

Producing charge by friction 摩擦生电

Rub a polythene rod with woollen cloth: the cloth loses electrons → becomes positive; the rod gains electrons → becomes negative.

用羊毛布摩擦聚乙烯棒：布失去电子 → 带正电；棒获得电子 → 带负电。

Becomes negative变为负电Becomes positive变为正电

NylonBalloon

Attraction & repulsion 吸引与排斥

Similar charges repel; different charges attract.

同性电荷相斥；异性电荷相吸。

  • Lightning ⚡ — friction charges clouds; negative charges at the cloud base jump to the ground or another cloud. 闪电 — 摩擦使云层带电；云底的负电荷跳向地面或另一云层。
  • Aeroplane tyres — specially designed to let accumulated charge flow to earth on landing. 飞机轮胎 — 特别设计使降落时积聚的电荷流向地面。
  • Petrol tankers — a metal chain lets charge flow to earth (rubber tyres block it). 油罐车 — 金属链让电荷流向地面（橡胶轮胎会阻隔电荷）。
  • Lightning conductor — sharp metal rod + earthed copper plate protects tall buildings. 避雷针 — 尖锐金属棒加接地铜板，保护高层建筑。
  • Faraday's cage — conducting enclosure that blocks an electromagnetic field (e.g. a car, a lift). 法拉第笼 — 能阻隔电磁场的导电外壳（例如汽车、电梯）。
7.1c

The Electroscope 验电器

Structure & purpose 结构与用途

An electroscope detects the presence of electric charges. It has a metal cap, insulator, metal rod, and a gold leaf attached at the bottom.

验电器用于检测电荷的存在。它由金属帽、绝缘体、金属杆和底部的金箔组成。

  • Neutral object touches cap → leaf does not diverge (no electron movement). 中性物体触碰金属帽 → 金箔不张开（没有电子移动）。
  • Charged object brought near/touches cap → leaf diverges (rod & leaf gain the same charge → repel). 带电物体靠近/触碰金属帽 → 金箔张开（金属杆与金箔带相同电荷而互相排斥）。
  • More charge accumulated → leaf diverges more. 积聚的电荷越多 → 金箔张开得越大。

Charging by induction 感应充电

Positive object near cap → electrons move from leaf to the cap (leaf becomes positive, diverges).

正电物体靠近金属帽 → 电子从金箔移向金属帽（金箔带正电，张开）。

Negative object near cap → electrons move from cap to the leaf (leaf becomes negative, diverges).

负电物体靠近金属帽 → 电子从金属帽移向金箔（金箔带负电，张开）。

Charges can be neutralised by touching a finger on the metal cap 用手指触碰金属帽可中和电荷
7.1d

Electric Current 电流

Definition & direction 定义与方向

Electric current (I) = the rate of flow of electric charges through a conductor. S.I. unit: ampere (A); also mA, μA.

电流（I） = 电荷通过导体的流动速率。国际单位：安培（A）；也用毫安（mA）、微安（μA）。

Current flows from positive → negative terminal. Electrons flow the opposite way (negative → positive).

电流方向：从正极流向负极。电子流动方向相反（负极流向正极）。

Measured with an ammeter, connected in series.

用安培计测量，以串联方式连接。

Voltage (potential difference) 电压（电位差）

Voltage (V) = energy required to move a unit charge between two points. S.I. unit: volt (V).

电压（V） = 移动单位电荷所需的能量。国际单位：伏特（V）。

Measured with a voltmeter, connected in parallel.

用电压计测量，以并联方式连接。

Resistance 电阻

Resistance (R) resists the flow of charge. Higher R → lower current. Unit: ohm (Ω).

电阻（R）阻碍电荷的流动。电阻越大 → 电流越小。单位：欧姆（Ω）。

Factor 因素Effect on resistance 对电阻的影响
Length ↑ 长度增加Resistance ↑ 电阻增加
Diameter ↑ 直径增加Resistance ↓ 电阻减少
Temperature ↑ 温度升高Resistance ↑ 电阻增加
Type of material 材料种类Different materials → different resistance 不同材料电阻不同

Types of resistors 电阻器种类

  • Fixed resistor — resistance cannot be adjusted. 固定电阻器 — 电阻不可调节。
  • Rheostat (variable resistor) — changeable resistance. 变阻器 — 电阻可调节。
  • LDR (light-dependent resistor) — resistance changes with light intensity. 光敏电阻 — 电阻随光照强度变化。

Why are iron/heater wires thick & short? Thicker, shorter wires → lower resistance → prevent overheating under large current.

为什么电熨斗、热水器的导线又粗又短？较粗较短的导线电阻较低，能防止大电流下过热。

7.1e

Ohm's Law 欧姆定律

The law 定律内容

Current flowing through a conductor is directly proportional to the voltage across it, provided temperature & physical conditions stay constant.

在温度和物理条件不变的情况下，导体中流过的电流与其两端的电压成正比。

V = I × R V = 电压（伏特） · I = 电流（安培） · R = 电阻（欧姆）

The gradient of an I vs V graph = 1/R

I-V 图的斜率 = 1/R

Worked example 例题

Find V across a 20 Ω resistor when I = 0.6 A. > V = IR > V = 0.6 × 20 > V = 12 V
A 12 V supply drives current through a 60 Ω resistor. Find I. > I = V / R > I = 12 / 60 > I = 0.2 A
7.2

Series & Parallel Circuits 串联与并联电路

Circuit components & symbols 电路元件与符号

Component 元件Function 功能
Dry cell 干电池Power source 电源
Switch 开关Connect/disconnect circuit 连接/断开电路
Ammeter (A) 安培计Measure current — series 测量电流 — 串联
Voltmeter (V) 电压计Measure voltage — parallel 测量电压 — 并联
Resistor / Rheostat 电阻器 / 变阻器Control current flow 控制电流
Fuse 保险丝Breaks circuit on overcurrent 过载时断开电路

SERIES 串联

  • Current same throughout: I = I₁ = I₂ = I₃ 电流处处相同
  • Voltage adds up: V = V₁ + V₂ + V₃ 电压相加
  • Resistance adds up: R = R₁ + R₂ + R₃ 电阻相加
  • One faulty appliance → whole circuit breaks 一个元件故障 → 整个电路断开
  • Controlled by one main switch 由一个总开关控制

PARALLEL 并联

  • Current adds up: I = I₁ + I₂ + I₃ 电流相加
  • Voltage same throughout: V = V₁ = V₂ = V₃ 电压处处相同
  • Resistance: 1/R = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + 1/R₃ 电阻倒数相加
  • One faulty appliance → others keep working 一个元件故障 → 其他仍可运作
  • Each can have its own switch 各支路可有独立开关

Series example 串联例题

3 resistors in series: 10Ω, 6Ω, 8Ω. Battery = 6V. > R = 10+6+8 = 24 Ω > V = IR → 6 = I × 24 > I = 0.25 A

Parallel example 并联例题

2 resistors in parallel: 4Ω, 12Ω. Battery = 1.5V. > 1/R = 1/4 + 1/12 = 1/3 > R = 3 Ω > I = V/R = 1.5/3 = 0.5 A

Why home wiring is parallel 为什么家居布线采用并联

All appliances get the same voltage as the main supply, and other appliances keep working even if one is faulty.

所有电器获得与主电源相同的电压；即使其中一个电器故障，其他电器仍能正常运作。

7.3

Magnetism & Electromagnets 磁学与电磁体

Magnetic field around conductors 导体周围的磁场

Straight wire — field forms concentric circles around the wire.

直导线 — 磁场形成围绕导线的同心圆。

Coiled / solenoid — field resembles a bar magnet's field.

螺线管 — 磁场类似条形磁铁的磁场。

Right-hand grip rule: thumb = current direction, fingers = field direction 右手定则：拇指指向电流方向，弯曲手指指向磁场方向

For a solenoid: thumb points to the north pole; fingers curl in the direction of current flow.

对于螺线管：拇指指向北极；手指弯曲方向为电流流动方向。

Factors affecting field strength 影响磁场强度的因素

  • Current magnitude ↑ → field stronger 电流强度增加 → 磁场增强
  • Number of coil turns ↑ → field stronger 线圈匝数增加 → 磁场增强
  • Soft iron core → field stronger (vs no core) 使用软铁芯 → 磁场增强（相较于无铁芯）

An electromagnet is a temporary magnet — magnetic only while current flows.

电磁体是一种暂时性磁铁——只有电流通过时才具有磁性。

Applications in daily life 日常生活中的应用

  • Cranes — move scrap metal 起重机 — 移动废金属
  • Magnetic door locks 磁性门锁
  • Compass — points to magnetic poles 指南针 — 指向磁极
  • Electric bell — hammer knocks repeatedly 电铃 — 锤子反复敲击
  • Power stations — generate electricity 发电站 — 产生电力
  • Credit/debit card magnetic strips 信用卡/借记卡磁条
