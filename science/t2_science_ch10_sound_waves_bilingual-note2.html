Core Concepts 核心概念

1. What is sound? 什么是声音？

Sound is a form of energy.

声音是一种能量。

Sound is produced by vibration.

声音由物体的振动产生。

2. Medium 介质

Sound needs a medium to travel. It can move through solids, liquids and gases.

声音需要介质才能传播，可通过固体、液体和气体传播。

Important: Sound cannot travel through a vacuum.
重点：声音不能在真空中传播。

3. Speed of sound 声速

Solid > Liquid > Gas

固体 > 液体 > 气体

Reason: particles in solids are packed closely, so vibrations are transferred more quickly.
原因：固体粒子排列紧密，振动能更快传递。

4. Human hearing range 人类听觉范围

20 Hz – 20 000 Hz

Below 20 Hz: infrasound. Above 20 000 Hz: ultrasound.

低于20 Hz：次声波。高于20 000 Hz：超声波。

Loudness and Pitch 响度与音调

Loudness 响度

Determined by amplitude.

由振幅决定。

Larger amplitude = louder sound
振幅越大 = 声音越响

Pitch 音调

Determined by frequency.

由频率决定。

Higher frequency = higher pitch
频率越高 = 音调越高

How to compare waveforms 如何比较波形

Feature 特征Look at 看什么Meaning 含义
Loudness 响度Height of wave 波峰高度Higher amplitude → louder 较大振幅→较响
Pitch 音调Number of waves in same time 相同时间内波数More waves → higher frequency → higher pitch 波数越多→频率越高→音调越高

Reflection and Absorption 声音反射与吸收

Hard, smooth surface 硬而光滑的表面

Reflects sound well.

能很好地反射声音。

Examples 例子：glass, concrete, metal
玻璃、混凝土、金属

Soft, rough surface 柔软而粗糙的表面

Absorbs sound well.

能很好地吸收声音。

Examples 例子：carpet, curtain, foam
地毯、窗帘、泡棉

Classic apparatus question 经典实验题

Question: Can student B hear the stopwatch through reflection from a glass sheet?

Model answer (EN): Yes. The sound of the stopwatch is reflected by the glass sheet toward student B.

标准答案（中）： 可以。秒表发出的声音被玻璃片反射，并传向学生B。

When glass is replaced by carpet:

Model answer (EN): The sound becomes very weak or cannot be heard because carpet is soft and rough, so it absorbs sound well.

标准答案（中）： 声音会变得很弱或听不见，因为地毯柔软且粗糙，能很好地吸收声音。

Applications of Sound 声音的应用

Stethoscope 听诊器

Helps doctors listen to heartbeat and breathing sounds.

帮助医生听取心跳和呼吸声。

Ultrasound 超声波

Sound with frequency above 20 000 Hz.

频率高于20 000 Hz的声波。

Used in medical imaging and pregnancy scans.
用于医学成像及胎儿扫描。

Sonar 声纳

Uses reflected sound to detect objects underwater.

利用反射声波探测水下物体。

Example: locating fish or measuring sea depth.
例如：寻找鱼群或测量海深。

Why does an ultrasound image form? 为什么能形成超声图像？

Different body tissues reflect ultrasound differently. The machine detects the reflected waves and converts them into an image.

不同身体组织对超声波的反射程度不同。仪器接收反射波后，把它转换成图像。

Exam Answering Guide 考试作答指南

Use this 3-part structure 使用“三段式”

Name the concept 写出概念
Reflection, absorption, amplitude, frequency, vibration, medium.
State the relationship 写出关系
“A larger amplitude produces a louder sound.”
Link to the situation 联系题目情境
“The carpet absorbs the sound, so less sound reaches the listener.”

Q1. What produces sound?

EN: Sound is produced by vibrating objects.
中：声音由振动物体产生。

Q2. Why is sound faster in solids?

EN: The particles in solids are closely packed, so vibrations are transferred quickly.
中：固体粒子排列紧密，因此振动能快速传递。

Q3. Which graph has the highest pitch?

EN: The graph with the most complete waves in the same time, because it has the highest frequency.
中：在相同时间内波数最多的图，因为它的频率最高。

Q4. State the Doppler effect.

EN: The Doppler effect is an apparent change in frequency caused by relative motion between the sound source and the observer.
中：多普勒效应是由于声源与观察者之间发生相对运动，导致观察到的频率改变。
