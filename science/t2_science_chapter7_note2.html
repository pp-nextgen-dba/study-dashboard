⚡ Electrical Energy and Electrostatic Charge / 电能与静电荷

Forms of energy / 能量形式

Electrical 电能 water heater, bulb, electronic devices

Kinetic 动能 running, cycling, flowing water, blowing air

Heat 热能 kettle, cooking, heater

Chemical 化学能 food, biomass, batteries

Elastic potential 弹性势能 bow, spring, stretched rubber band

Sources of energy / 能源

Main source on Earth: The Sun. 地球主要能源：太阳。

Renewable examples: solar, hydro, wind, biomass. 可再生：太阳能、水力、风能、生物质。

Non-renewable examples: fossil fuels and radioactive substances. 不可再生：化石燃料和放射性物质。

Static charge by rubbing / 摩擦产生静电

  1. Objects are initially neutral. 物体起初通常是中性的。
  2. When rubbed, electrons transfer; protons do not move. 摩擦时只有电子转移，质子不移动。
  3. Object gaining electrons becomes negative. 得到电子的物体带负电。
  4. Object losing electrons becomes positive. 失去电子的物体带正电。

Rules of charges / 电荷规则

Same charges repel. 同种电荷相斥。

Opposite charges attract. 异种电荷相吸。

Electroscope: detects the presence of electric charge. 验电器：用来探测物体是否带电。

Exam note: For questions like “plastic comb becomes negatively charged”, answer: electrons move from the cloth to the plastic comb, so the comb has more electrons than protons.
考试答法：电子从布转移到塑料梳，所以塑料梳电子多于质子，带负电。

🔋 Current, Voltage, Resistance and Ohm’s Law / 电流、电压、电阻与欧姆定律

Current / 电流

Current is the rate of flow of electric charges through a conductor. 电流是电荷通过导体的流动速率。

Measured by: Ammeter. 测量工具：安培计。

Unit: ampere, A. 单位：安培。

Voltage / 电压

Voltage is the potential difference between two points. 电压是两点之间的电势差。

Measured by: Voltmeter. 测量工具：伏特计。

Unit: volt, V. 单位：伏特。

Resistance / 电阻

Resistance limits or resists the flow of electric current. 电阻会限制或阻碍电流流动。

Unit: ohm, Ω. 单位：欧姆。

Ohm’s Law / 欧姆定律

Current is directly proportional to voltage when temperature and other physical conditions are constant.

在温度和其他物理条件保持不变时，电流与电压成正比。

V = I × R
I = V / R
R = V / I
Relationship: More dry cells → higher voltage → higher current → bulb becomes brighter.
关系：干电池越多 → 电压越高 → 电流越大 → 灯泡越亮。

💡 Series Circuit and Parallel Circuit / 串联与并联电路

Series circuit / 串联电路

Battery ─ Bulb ─ Bulb ─ Bulb ─ back

Current: same through each component. 电流：每个元件相同。

Voltage: shared by components. 电压：由元件分配。

Resistance: total resistance is the sum.

I = I₁ = I₂
V = V₁ + V₂
R = R₁ + R₂ + R₃

Disadvantage: If one component breaks, the whole circuit stops. 缺点：一个元件坏了，整个电路停止。

Parallel circuit / 并联电路

┌─ Bulb ─┐ Battery ─┼─ Bulb ─┼─ back └─ Bulb ─┘

Voltage: same across each branch. 电压：每个支路相同。

Current: total current is the sum of branch currents. 电流：总电流等于各支路电流之和。

Resistance: effective resistance is smaller than any branch resistance.

I = I₁ + I₂
V = V₁ = V₂
1/R = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂

Advantage: If one bulb burns out, others can still light up. 优点：一个灯泡坏了，其他灯泡仍可亮。

Resistance examples / 电阻计算例子

Set A series: R = 5 Ω + 3 Ω + 4 Ω = 12 Ω.

Set B parallel: 1/R = 1/6 + 1/4 = 5/12, so R = 2.4 Ω.

串联总电阻会变大；并联有效电阻会变小。

🧲 Magnetism / 磁学

Properties of a magnet / 磁铁性质

  • Attracts magnetic materials. 吸引磁性材料。
  • Has north and south poles. 有北极和南极。
  • Like poles repel, unlike poles attract. 同极相斥，异极相吸。
  • Shows north-south direction when suspended freely. 自由悬挂时指向南北方向。

Magnetic field / 磁场

Magnetic field is the area around a magnet where magnetic force exists. 磁场是磁铁周围存在磁力作用的区域。

Magnetic field is strongest near the poles. 磁极附近磁场最强。

Magnetic field lines / 磁力线

  • Never meet or cross. 不会相交或重叠。
  • Outside the magnet, they go from north pole to south pole. 在磁铁外部，由北极指向南极。
  • Closer lines mean stronger field. 线越密，磁场越强。

Bar magnet field / 条形磁铁磁场

🧲⚡ Electromagnetism / 电磁学

What is an electromagnet? / 什么是电磁铁？

An electromagnet is a temporary magnet produced when electric current flows through a coil of wire. 电磁铁是电流通过线圈时产生的暂时性磁铁。

Direction of magnetic field / 磁场方向

The direction depends on the direction of electric current. Use the right-hand grip rule. 磁场方向取决于电流方向。可使用右手握拳定则。

Thumb = current direction; curled fingers = magnetic field direction. 拇指代表电流方向；弯曲手指代表磁场方向。

Field pattern by conductor / 导体形状影响磁场图案

  • Straight wire: circular field lines around wire. 直导线：磁力线呈圆形围绕导线。
  • Coiled wire: field becomes stronger and more concentrated. 线圈：磁场更集中更强。
  • Solenoid: magnetic field resembles a bar magnet. 螺线管：磁场像条形磁铁。

Factors increasing strength / 增强电磁铁的方法

  • Increase current. 增加电流。
  • Increase number of turns of coil. 增加线圈匝数。
  • Use an iron core/iron nail/iron rod. 使用铁芯/铁钉/铁棒。

Experiment language / 实验答题句型

Manipulated variable: current OR number of turns of coil. 操纵变量：电流 或 线圈匝数。

Responding variable: number of pins/paper clips attracted. 反应变量：被吸引的大头针/回形针数量。

Hypothesis: The more the number of turns of coil, the stronger the magnetic field. 假设：线圈匝数越多，磁场越强。

Operational definition: Strength of magnetic field is shown by the number of pins/paper clips attracted. 操作性定义：磁场强度以被吸引的大头针/回形针数量表示。

🛠 Applications / 应用题重点

Magnetic lock / 磁力门锁

When the switch is turned on, current flows through the coil. The coil becomes an electromagnet and attracts the iron bolt, pulling it away so the door can open.

开关闭合后，电流通过线圈，线圈变成电磁铁，吸引铁栓，使铁栓被拉开，门就能打开。

Electric motor / 电动机

A stronger magnetic field gives a stronger turning effect and higher speed. More coil turns and more batteries usually produce a stronger motor effect.

磁场越强，转动效果越强，速度越快。线圈匝数更多、电池更多，通常能产生更强的电动机效果。

Fire alarm uses parallel circuit / 火警系统用并联电路

Parallel circuit allows each alarm/bell to work independently. If one part fails, the others can still operate.

并联电路让每个警报器/铃可以独立工作。即使其中一个损坏，其他仍可操作。

Dusty TV screen / 电视屏幕容易沾灰尘

Static charges on the screen attract dust. Use microfibre cloth or anti-static cloth to reduce static charge.

电视屏幕上的静电会吸引灰尘。可使用超细纤维布或防静电布来减少静电。

✅ Practice and Quick Answers / 练习与快速答案

1. Name the energy forms: kettle, running boy, archer with bow.
Kettle: heat energy. Running boy: kinetic energy. Archer with bow: elastic potential energy.
水壶：热能；跑步：动能；拉弓：弹性势能。
2. Why does a balloon become negatively charged after rubbing with towel?
Electrons are transferred from the towel to the balloon. The balloon has more electrons than protons, so it becomes negatively charged.
电子从毛巾转移到气球，气球电子多于质子，所以带负电。
3. Series circuit: R1 = 5 Ω, R2 = 3 Ω, R3 = 4 Ω. Find total resistance.
R = 5 + 3 + 4 = 12 Ω. 串联电阻相加。
4. Parallel circuit: R1 = 4 Ω, R2 = 6 Ω. Find effective resistance.
1/R = 1/4 + 1/6 = 5/12, so R = 2.4 Ω. 并联有效电阻小于任何一个支路电阻。
5. Magnetic field lines: write two characteristics.
They never meet or cross. They begin from the north pole and end at the south pole outside the magnet.
磁力线不会相交；在磁铁外部由北极指向南极。
6. In an electromagnet experiment, what happens if current increases?
More pins/paper clips are attracted because the magnetic field becomes stronger.
被吸引的大头针/回形针数量增加，因为磁场变强。
7. Choose faster electric motor: Fan P has 10 coil turns and 1 battery; Fan Q has 50 coil turns and 2 batteries.
Choose Fan Q. It has more coil turns and more batteries, so it produces a stronger magnetic field and rotates faster.
选择风扇 Q。它有更多线圈匝数和更多电池，磁场更强，转得更快。
8. Correct measuring equipment for current and voltage.
Current is measured by an ammeter. Voltage is measured by a voltmeter.
电流用安培计测量；电压用伏特计测量。

📝 Exam Answer Templates / 考试答题模板

Explain static charge / 解释静电

When two objects are rubbed together, electrons are transferred from one object to another. The object that gains electrons becomes negatively charged.

当两个物体互相摩擦时，电子从一个物体转移到另一个物体。得到电子的物体会带负电。

Compare series and parallel / 比较串联与并联

In a series circuit, all components are connected in one path and share the current. In a parallel circuit, components are connected in different branches and each branch can operate independently.

串联电路只有一条路径；并联电路有多个支路，各支路可独立工作。

Explain stronger electromagnet / 解释电磁铁增强

Increasing the current or the number of turns of coil increases the strength of the magnetic field. Therefore, more paper clips can be attracted.

增加电流或线圈匝数会增强磁场，因此能吸引更多回形针。

Relationship of field lines and strength / 磁力线与强度关系

The closer the magnetic field lines are to one another, the stronger the magnetic field.

磁力线越密，磁场越强。
