Part 1 · Hostile / Belligerent 敌意 / 好斗型

Their anger = their own stress/insecurity, not your fault. Stay calm, set boundaries. 对方的敌意大多来自自身压力/自卑，不是你的错。保持冷静，划清底线。

Boss · 上司
Narcissist
自恋型
Respect yourself, confirm in writing, focus on goals.
保持自尊，书面确认，围绕目标做事。
Bully
霸道型
Do not fear; confidence removes their power.
别示弱；自信能削弱他的气势。
Ruthless
冷酷型
Demand respect, keep records, use HR.
要求尊重，留好证据，善用公司制度。
Colleague · 同事

让他发泄，再提供帮助。

保持专业，只谈工作，不接人身攻击。

保存记录，正式举报。

客气友善，肯定他的优点。

提前准备回应，保持从容。

Part 2 · Arrogant / Pushy 傲慢 / 强势型

Their arrogance = fear of losing control / insecurity. Work with their ego, not against it. 傲慢强势 = 害怕失控/内心不安。顺着他的自尊，比硬刚更有效。

Boss · 上司
Dictator
独裁型
Give regular updates, reduce micromanaging.
主动汇报，减少过度干预。
Fame Claimer
抢功型
Share credit openly, keep your own records.
公开分享功劳，私下保留证据。
Blocker
保守型
Frame ideas as theirs, use facts.
把方案说成"完善您的思路"，用数据说话。
Colleague · 同事
Moocher

温和但坚定地拒绝。
Crusher
压制型
Assert your role clearly.
明确自己的权责范围。
Knee-Jerker
冲动型
Ask details, use facts to slow them down.
追问细节，用事实降温。
Competitor
竞争型

真诚肯定他的长处。
Subordinate · 下属

明确规则+后果。

Part 3 · Deceitful / Underhanded 虚伪 / 耍手段型

Their dishonesty = self-protection / control. Protect yourself with clarity + written proof. 虚伪耍手段 = 自保/掌控欲。用清晰沟通+书面证据保护自己。

Rule: If it's not written, it's not real.
原则：没白纸黑字，就不算数。
Field Manual · 黄金法则

5 Golden Rules

五条黄金法则
1
Don't take it personally — it's about them, not you.
别往心里去 — 是他们的问题，不是你的错。
2
Stay calm, not emotional — anger makes you lose control.
保持冷静 — 情绪化只会让你被动。
3
Set clear boundaries — say "no" firmly but politely.
划清边界 — 温和但坚定地说"不"。
4
Keep records — emails, notes, proof = your best defense.
留好证据 — 邮件、记录、凭证就是保障。
5
Focus on results, not fights — win the situation, not the argument.
对事不对人 — 要解决问题，不是争输赢。
